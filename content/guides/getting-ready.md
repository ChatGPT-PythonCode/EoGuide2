After completing Import Complete, speak with the Import Assistant
2. He will ask you to retrieve the Teleport Key
3. If you’re not sure how to use the Broken Wrench he gives you, “No (Tutorial)” option
4. Equip your Broken Wrench and enter the next room to the south (down)
5. Once inside, turn east (right) and go down the stairs
6. Defeat Stealmites until one of them drops a Teleport Key
7. Once you have the Teleport Key, return to the Import Assistant
8. Give him the key and receive your reward of 200 EXP
9. To leave, enter the next room to the north (up)
10. Stand on the back wall against the teleporter
11. The teleporter will send you to Aeven North
