Arena King is a quest that tasks you with assisting Unknown in Aeven. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Aeven
Arena King
Location:
Aeven Castle Garden
Quest Giver:
Arena Doorkeeper
Level Requirement:
Reward:
200,000 exp and 1,000 eons
Head to the Arena, either by walking from Aeven or clicking on the social games tab on the left side of your game
2. Talk to the Arena Doorkeeper
3. He asks you to play 100 matches
4. Head inside and participate in 100 arena matches! You can track your progress on your Q tab at the top left of your game
5. Go back to the Arena Doorkeeper after playing 100 matches to obtain your reward of 200,000 exp and 1,000 eon
6. After completing this quest, it turns into a weekly quest that you can complete 1x a week for 40,000 exp and 500 eons
