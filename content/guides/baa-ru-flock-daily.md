Baa-ru Flock (daily) is a quest that tasks you with assisting Cara in Aeven. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

Note:

2. Note:
The quest
Strange Neighbours
is a prerequisite to start this quest
3. WHAT YOU'LL NEED:
1 Super Sheep Food (1 Shiny Puffyweed and 1 Shiny Chewyherb)
Head to Aeven West and find the Baru Shepherd
4. Speak to the Baru Shepherd
5. He asks you to bring him 1 Super Sheep Food from Nostalgia Island
6. To craft Super Sheep Food, you’ll need:
·      1 Shiny Puffyweed
·      1 Shiny Chewyherb
After speaking with the Baru Shepherd, gather at least 1 Puffyweed north-west of his location
7. If you forget this step, you can collect Puffyweed around Aeven or the surrounding maps
8. Next, travel to Nostalgia Island:
·      Head east back into Aeven, then south into the Aeven Port
9. ·      Speak to the Boat Captain and select Nostalgia Port
10. Once on Nostalgia Island, collect 1 Chewyherb from a resource node
11. (You may want to gather extra if planning multiple crafts.)
After collecting your Chewyherbs, return to Aeven by speaking to the Boat Captain at Nostalgia Port
12. Go to the Aeven Market, talk to Cara Craft Merchant and purchase 30–40 Empty Bucket
13. Travel to the Stronghold Woods east of Aeven and use the well by the road
14. ·      Each time you fill an Empty Bucket, you have a 10% chance to obtain a Bucket of Pure Water
·      Repeat until you collect 2 Buckets of Pure Water
After acquiring 2 Buckets of Pure Water, return to the Aeven Market and talk to Hilda Herbs to craft 1 Shiny Puffyweed and 1 Shiny Chewyherb
15. Return to Nostalgia Island
16. From the port, go south one map and enter the southernmost building
17. Inside, speak to Buddy Chef and craft 1 Super Sheep Food
18. Finally, head back to the Baru Shepherd in Aeven West
19. He’ll then reward you with 2,000 exp and 1 Baru Flock
20. You will need 20 baru flocks to craft a baru or baruta
21. You can craft it at the Aeven Blacksmith for 3 casual textile, 3 beginner sewing kits, and 20 baru flock (at the time of writing this guide.) You can also use
#item
baru in game to view the current crafting ingredients
