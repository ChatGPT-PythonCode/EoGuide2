5. Enter the Aeven Witch Shop and talk to the Witch
During the dialogue, select the following options in order:
·       Hello fellow witch
6. ·       I studied witchcraft for years
7. ·       What is the missing ingredient?
·       Yes, I knew that all along
8. Exit the shop and speak with Ayla again
9. She will explain that Moonpetals blossom at Aeven Top
10. Head to Aeven Top by going to the Aeven Top Lift, north of Aeven
11. Take the next lift just north of you
12. Collect the Moonpetal from the ground
13. Return to Ayla outside the Witch Shop in Aeven
14. Ayla thanks you and rewards you with 2,200 exp and 100 eons
