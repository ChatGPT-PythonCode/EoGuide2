## Requirements

- Level 3+


## Steps

Happy Berries is the third quest the player should encounter. It has players kill their first monsters to complete the quest. The quest finishes with teaching the player how to harvest berries. The quest can be started by talking to the Happy Berry Picker NPC at the Decayed Temple just outside the mine.


1. Talk to Happy Berry Picker
2. Get rid of 5 crows
3. Talk to Happy Berry Picker

## Walkthrough
Begin the quest by talking to the Happy Berry Picker NPC at the Decayed Temple just outside the mine.
Talk to Happy Berry Picker
Select the "I will help you" option.
Get rid of 5 crows
Kill 5 crows in the area.
Talk to Happy Berry Picker
Talk to the Happy Berry Picker and finish the conversation to complete the quest.
