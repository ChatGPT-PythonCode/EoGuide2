Nibby's Scavenge (daily) is a quest given by Nibby in Quest Giver:. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. It's recommended to start the quest by activating it in your Quest Window rather than traveling to Nibby
2. Open your quest window and click on the daily tab
3. Select Nibby's Scavenge (daily) to active it
4. (Go to step 5 if you're activating the quest through the Quest Window)
5. Once you reach the island head north off the docks and continue until you find Nibby's Tree House
6. Once your there walk onto the rope hanging from the tree to enter Nibby's Tree House
7. Speaker with Nibby and she will ask for a set number of random items
8. If you start the quest and don't like the item required to complete it, then wait the quest items rotate every 6 hours
9. Just abort the quest and wait 6 hours to retry it
10. 5.Once you have your list from Nibby go collect the proper amount of the requested item and return to Nibby
11. Here is a list of things she may ask for:
   50 twigs
   50 iron ore
   50 wood logs
   5 flower bouquets
   20 Vlinder wings
   25 ice gems
   25 wild roots
   20 X-Markers
   20 Old Shoes
   Panda Secret (weekly)
   Making Buddies (Daily)
