**Location:** Waku Treehouse  
**Quest Giver:** Scared Warrior  
**Level Requirement:** 20  
**Rewards:** 10,000 exp

Scared is a quest given by Scared Warrior in Waku Treehouse. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. 2 maps north of the Waku Port, you'll find a cave at the northeast corner at the map at x:8, y:6
2. Talk to the Scared Warrior
3. He asks you to kill 1 bigtooth within the cave
4. Make your way in the cave and kill a bigtooth
5. Careful - there is a short path with biters guarding it and they hit pretty hard
6. Talk to him again to activate the next quest, Big Claws
   Big Teeth
   Big Claws

## Completion

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **Unknown**.
