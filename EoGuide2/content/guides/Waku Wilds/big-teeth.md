**Location:** Waku Treehouse  
**Quest Giver:** Scared Warrior  
**Level Requirement:** 20  
**Rewards:** 44,000 exp and 1 Jungle Shield

Big Teeth is a quest given by Scared Warrior in Waku Treehouse. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. 2 maps north of the Waku Port, you'll find a cave at the northeast corner at the map at x:8, y:6
2. The Scared Warrior asks you for 90 Big Teeth in exchange for his Jungle Shield
3. Collect 90 Big Teeth
4. You can buy them from other players or grind the drops at bigtooth
5. At the time of writing this (October 19, 2025) the drop rate is 11%
6. It's quite a long grind if you get all of the drops manually
