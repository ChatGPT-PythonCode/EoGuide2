**Location:** Waku Treehouse  
**Quest Giver:** Scared Warrior  
**Level Requirement:** 20  
**Rewards:** 24,000 exp and 1 Spear

Big Claws is a quest given by Scared Warrior in Waku Treehouse. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. 2 maps north of the Waku Port, you'll find a cave at the northeast corner at the map at x:8, y:6
2. Talk to the Scared Warrior
3. He asks you to bring 10 big claws
4. Go in the cave and get 10 claws
5. At the time of writing this (September 23 2025) the drop rate is 19% for claws, so
   you may be there quite a while
6. You
   can also buy them from other players
7. While you are grinding for claws, remember to keep the teeth as you will need them for the next quest
   Big Teeth
   Scared

## Completion

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **Unknown**.
