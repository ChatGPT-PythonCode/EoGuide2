**Location:** Wacku Deep West  
**Quest Giver:** Furry Furgy  
**Level Requirement:** 36  
**Rewards:** 6,000 exp

Ten Furs Later (rep) is a quest given by Furry Furgy in Wacku Deep West. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Talk to Furry Furgy again to start it
2. He'll ask you to bring him 10 more ape fur
3. Go around the map defeating Wild Apes again until you collect 10 Ape Fur
4. (Ape Fur has a 12% chance to drop per kill)
   Turkey
   Daily Soupy (daily)
   Big Teeth

## Completion

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **Unknown**.
