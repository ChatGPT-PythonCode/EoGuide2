Jungle Run is a quest given by Monkey Explorer in Waku Deep Wilds. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Once you're at the Waku port, turn left and go inside the cave
2. Follow the video below to find the entrance to the maze
3. Tips:
   Before doing the maze, right click the top of your client, go to input, and turn on ghostable npcs
4. Biters move fast and might block you - ghost npc avoids the npcs blocking you
5. There are safe zones between the biters - when you get hit, just sit-heal (or use potions.)
   The Monkey Explorer asks you to report his findings to the Wise Monkey in Monkey Island
