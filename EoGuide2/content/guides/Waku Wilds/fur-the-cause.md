**Location:** Wacku Deep West  
**Quest Giver:** Furry Furgy  
**Level Requirement:** Unknown  
**Rewards:** 90,000 exp

Fur the Cause is a quest given by Furry Furgy in Wacku Deep West. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Go to Wacku Deep West by first going to the Aeven Port and talking to the Boat Captain and travel to Wacku Wilds Port
2. Once you're off the boat go north until your off the docks then head west until you reach the cave
3. Enter the cave then travel to the west side and cross the wooden bridge
4. Once leaving the cave head west and find Furry Furgy
5. He will ask you to bring him 10 Ape Fur
6. Walk the map killing apes until you have collected 10 ape fur
7. Warning
   - Apes will agro onto you if they see you attacking another Ape next to them
