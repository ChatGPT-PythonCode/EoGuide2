Josand
Suspicious PotsLocation: Josand Big MansionQuest Giver: Shubba DunaLevel Requirement:  26Reward: 55,000 exp and 300 eonsGo to the Josland Big Mansion, located on the north side of Josand Town.  Talk to Shubba Duna to start the quest.Shubba Duna explains that strange noises and missing items have been reported in people’s homes, and he asks you to investigate.Leave the mansion and use the #range command outside to locate the coordinates of a Suspicious Pot. Go to the marked coordinates with your mini-map open.Interact with the Suspicious Pot. A Ninja Spy will jump out and attack you — defeat the Ninja Spy.Return to Shubba Duna and report the discovery of the Spy Ninjas. Shubba Duna asks you to continue investigating. Go back outside, use #range again, and locate another Suspicious Pot.Interact with the pot and defeat the Spy Ninja once again.Return to Shubba Duna, who thanks you for your help and rewards you with 55,000 exp and 300 eons.
