1.From the Aeven spawn area, go up the elevator
3. This will take you to the Aeven airdock
4. Purchase an air ticket and go to Snowlandia
5. Once you arrive at the Snowlandia port, head west (left) and go down the stairs to enter Snowlandia Town
6. Once you're at Snowlandia Town - it's time to find the Yuki Snowflake NPC
7. This map is pretty large - Yuki Snowflake is located inside a house on the southwestern side of the map, at coordinates 24,51
8. Turn on your minimap by pressing Tab on your keyboard to see the coordinates on the map
9. 2.Talk to Yuki
10. He asks you to "inspect" (this means sit, or F11, on)  several sleighs around Snowlandia town, starting with one infront of Snowlandia Bank
11. 3.The first brown sleigh is infront of Snowlandia bank (around the corner from Yuki Snowflake's house) at coordinates 17,39
12. Sit down on it, and it will prompt you to go back to Yuki
13. 4.Walk back to Yuki
14. He asks you to examine the brown sleigh on the left side of the lake
15. 5.Go outside and sit down on the sleigh on the left of the lake at coordinates 24,34
16. 6.Walk back to Yuki
17. He asks you to inspect the brown sleigh on the upper side of the lake
18. 7.Sit down on the brown sleigh located on the north side of the lake at coordinates 39,23
19. 8.Walk back to Yuki
20. He asks you to sit down on the brown sleigh on the south side of the frozen lake
21. 9.Sit down on the brown sleigh on the south side of the frozen lake at coordinates 42,55
22. 10.Walk back to Yuki
23. He asks you to inspect the red sleigh directly behind his house
24. 11.Sit on the red sleigh behind Yuki's house at coordinates 11,51
25. 12.Once you walk back (again.........) to Yuki, he asks you to sit on the red sleigh on the north side of the lake
26. 13.Sit on the red sleigh on the north side of the lake at coordinates 41,22
27. 14.Go back to Yuki one last time and receive your reward of 28,000 exp and 900 eons
