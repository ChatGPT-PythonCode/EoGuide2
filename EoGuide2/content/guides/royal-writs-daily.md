Aeven
Royal Writs (daily)Location: Aeven EntombmentQuest Giver: Royal ScoutLevel Requirement:  Level 30Reward: 2,000 exp and 1 Royal WritsNote: This is the only way to acquire Royal Writs.  They are used for crafting Royal Arch Armor which requires 40 Royal Writs. The quest Skeleton Sweep is a prerequisite to start this questHead to the Aeven Entombment and find the Royal Scout.The Royal Scout will ask you to defeat 10 Grave Archers within the Aeven Catacombs.Enter the Aeven Catacombs and defeat 10 Grave Archers.Once you've defeated 10 Grave Archers return to the Royal Writs to claim your reward of 2,000 exp and 1 Royal Writs.Go North 2 Maps and enter the Aeven Castle and speak with the Royal Writkeeper to craft your Royal Arch Armor.
