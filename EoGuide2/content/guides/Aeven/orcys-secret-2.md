Orcy's Secret 2 is part of the Orcy's Secret 1 quest chain. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Aeven
   Orcy's Secret 2
   Starting Point: Shadow Desert (Aeven)
   Repeatable: NO
   Start Level: 20
   Max Level: NONE
   EXP: 8000
   EON: 0
2. First we go to the Baby Orc in the shadow desert
3. (Hopefully you still have your shrink key if not craft another)
4. Go inside the tiny door and speak with Baby Orcy
5. In the dialog select "what did you give me", "Do you want this item?" and "I will find it!"
6. Once you're done talking with Baby Orcy go back to Aeven and speak with Shady next to the wood shop
7. He will tell you he sold the pacifier to someone in the Blobsie Garden and you need to speak with them
8. Now we go to the Blobsie Garden, it can be found north of Aeven, then east of the Milk Farm
9. Once at the blobsie garden, go all the way straight and speak with Florabella
10. She will ask for a trade in order to make a new pacifier
11. Accept the deal and now prepare to spend a eterinty at the garden as you must now kill 500 Blobsie to continue
12. Now walk the map your on and kill 500 blobsie once you are done return to Florabella and you will receive the Ocrys Pacy 2
13. Once you have the Pacy, return to Baby Orcy and Then go to aeven falls
14. (North side of bat cave)
15. Once you exit the cave at aeven falls continue north over the bridge to the far north side and in between the two water falls you will see a tree with a cave
16. Walk into the cave and up to the altar and click on it to learn the spell "Small Heal"
