Reconstruction (rep) is a quest given by Foreman in Aeven (outside Fairy Soda Pub). Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Head to the Aeven Fairy Soda Pub and talk to the Foreman standing outside
2. The Foreman asks you to travel to the Stronghold to arrange a wood shipment
3. Go to the Stronghold Office and speak with the Stronghold Chief
4. The Stronghold chief gives you a Delivery Receipt and sends you back to the Foreman
5. Return to the Foreman with the Delivery Receipt
6. He then asks you to visit the Aeven Port to check on a shipment of roof tiles
7. Head south to the Aeven Port and find the Port Master
8. The Port Master gives you another Delivery Receipt and sends you back to the Foreman
9. Return to the Foreman one final time
