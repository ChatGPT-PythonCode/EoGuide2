Arena Addict (weekly) is a quest in Outside of Castle. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Aeven
   Arena Addict (weekly)
   Frequency:
   1x a week
   Outside of Castle
   Note:
   The quest
   Arena King
   is a prerequisite to unlocking this weekly quest
2. Head to the Arena to find the Arena Doorkeeper NPC, either by walking from Aeven or clicking on the social games tab on the left side of your game
3. You can also activate this weekly quest on your Q tab on the top left of your game
4. Talk to the Arena Doorkeeper
5. He asks you to play 100 matches
6. Head inside and participate in 100 arena matches! You can track your progress on your Q tab at the top left of your game
   Devpost 4.33
   Aeven
   Weekly Quest
   Arena King

## Completion

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **40,000 EXP, 500 Eons**.
