**Location:** Bunker 5 (Factory Control)  
**Quest Giver:** Inventor  
**Level Requirement:** 1  
**Rewards:** 1,580 exp

Sample is a quest given by Inventor in Bunker 5 (Factory Control). Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Aeven
   Sample
   Bunker 5 (Factory Control)
   Inventor
   Level 1 (based off prerequisite quest)
   1,580 exp
   Note:
   The quest
   Unlocked
   (minimum level 1) is a prerequisite to start this quest
2. WHAT YOU'LL NEED:
   1 Iron (crafted from 8 Iron Ore)
   Go to Bunker 5 in the Shadow Desert
3. To get there:
   ·       Travel north of Aeven into the Aeven Mines
4. ·       Take the northeast exit
5. ·       From there, head east through the Shadow Desert until you reach Bunker 5
6. Once there, enter Bunker 5 and talk to the Inventor to start the quest
   The Inventor explains he is still analyzing data from the impact site but needs to take a sample from it
7. He asks you to help him find his sampler, which he lost in Bunker 1
8. Travel to Bunker 1 by leaving Bunker 5 and going west one map then north one map
9. Inside follow the path west then north into a room
10. You will find the Broken Sampler on the floor behind several overturned tables
11. Pick up the Broken Sampler, then return to the Inventor in Bunker 5
12. The Inventor now tasks you with gathering 1 Iron to repair the sampler
13. Go into the Aeven Mines and collect 8 Iron Ore
14. Take the ore to the Aeven Forge and craft 1 Iron
15. With the Iron, return to Bunker 5
16. Inside Bunker 5, craft the Energy Sampler on the Craft Table
17. After crafting the Energy Sampler, talk to the Inventor
18. The Inventor thanks you for your excellent work, then asks you to use the Energy Sampler on the beam at the Aeven Impact Site to collect a sample
19. Go to the Aeven Impact Site
20. (Use the teleporter north of the Inventor’s room for a shortcut)
21. Stand next to the beam
22. An in-game message will appear instructing you to return to the Inventor
23. Go back to Bunker 5 and talk to the Inventor
24. The Inventor confirms the sample looks good and informs you his scanners have detected another energy source at the Aeven Stronghold
   Travel to the Aeven Stronghold Impact Site
25. (Use the teleporter north of the Inventor’s room for a shortcut, then head east two maps)
26. Stand next to the beam
27. An in-game message will appear instructing you to return to the Inventor
28. Return to Bunker 5 and speak to the Inventor
29. The Inventor will share his findings with you
30. He explains he needs to triple-check the results and suggests you contact the Aeven Group, leading into the next quest
   Aeven
   Turkey
   Unlocked
