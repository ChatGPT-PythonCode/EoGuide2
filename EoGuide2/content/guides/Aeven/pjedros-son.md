**Location:** Aeven Falls  
**Quest Giver:** Pjedro Junior  
**Level Requirement:** 30  
**Rewards:** 201,100 exp

Pjedro's Son is a quest given by Pjedro Junior in Aeven Falls. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. To start the quest go to the Aeven Inn by going 1 map to the east of Aeven
2. Once inside, talk to Pjedro Junior
3. Shocking - his son is missing
4. and he fell into a deep cave while on his way to Stronghold
5. He then tells you to go inside the cave above Green Lake
6. Go inside the Green Lake Cave and turn to the right to find the rescue leader
7. Talk to the Rescue Leader
8. He will send you to talk to the Outpost Captain at Moonmurks
9. Talk to the Outpost Captain at Moonmurks, he is directly above the port and receive permission
10. Go back to the cave above Green Lake and talk to the rescue leader
11. From there, you can go down the rope
12. it's time to find Pjedro
13. Find your way through the map while evading the death worms
14. There are some safe spots where you can sit and heal, or you can use potions
15. Remember to right click your client, go to input, and turn on ghostable NPCs so the worms don't get in your way
16. After going through the maze, talk to Pjedro's Son
17. Then, talk to the rescue leader
