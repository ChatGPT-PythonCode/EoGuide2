**Location:** Aeven Farmlet (Aeven House)  
**Quest Giver:** Dolly Dusty  
**Level Requirement:** 5  
**Rewards:** 700 exp

Spider Cleanup (rep) is a quest given by Dolly Dusty in Aeven Farmlet (Aeven House). Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Head one map west of Aeven and go inside the first building you see
2. Speak with Dolly Dusty
3. She will ask you to clean up 10 Little Spiders from the basement
4. Go down the stairs into the basement and pick up 10 Little Spiders
5. Once you have gathered 10 Little Spiders return to Dolly Dusty and claim your prize of 700 exp
