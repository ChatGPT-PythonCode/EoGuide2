Aevenomics-1 is a quest given by Aeven Manager in Aeven. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Aeven
   Aevenomics-1
2. The primary purpose of this quest line is for players to become familiar with many of the NPCs surrounding Aeven
3. Aevenomics-1 -> Aevenomics-2 -> Aevenomics-3
   Aeven
   Aeven Manager
   1000 exp and 20 eons
   Go to the northern side of Aeven and talk to the Aeven Manager to start the quest
4. Tell him you’re looking for a job
5. He tasks you with inquiring with multiple vendors about their businesses
6. Reference the map to locate all vendors you’ll need to speak to
7. First, head east and talk to Happy Berry Mike
8. Discuss finances with him
9. Once finished, Mike will give you his report
10. Return to the Aeven Manager and deliver Mike’s report
11. Next, he sends you to speak with Ramdon Goresy
12. Go to the easternmost side of the market and discuss finances with Ramdon Goresy
13. After receiving his report, return to the Aeven Manager
14. The Aeven Manager now asks you to talk with Creative Caroline
15. Head south and discuss finances with Creative Caroline
16. Once you have her report, return to the Aeven Manager
17. Next, he directs you to Bank Daddy
18. Cross the street, enter the bank (second house south once you’re on the road), and discuss finances with Bank Daddy
19. After getting his report, return to the Aeven Manager
20. He’ll then assign you one last task
21. Go to the Aeven Lawyer to file the reports
22. Once the reports are filed, return to the Aeven Manager one final time

## Completion

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **1000 EXP, 20 Eons**.
