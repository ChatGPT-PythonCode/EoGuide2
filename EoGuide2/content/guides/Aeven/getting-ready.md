**Location:** Bunker 5 (Factory Control)  
**Quest Giver:** Import Assistant  
**Level Requirement:** 1  
**Rewards:** 200 exp, Broken Wrench and Teleport Access

Getting Ready is a quest given by Import Assistant in Bunker 5 (Factory Control). Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Aeven
   Getting Ready
   Bunker 5 (Factory Control)
   Import Assistant
   Level 1
   200 exp, Broken Wrench and Teleport Access
   After completing Import Complete, speak with the Import Assistant
2. He will ask you to retrieve the Teleport Key
3. If you’re not sure how to use the Broken Wrench he gives you, “No (Tutorial)” option
4. Equip your Broken Wrench and enter the next room to the south (down)
5. Once inside, turn east (right) and go down the stairs
6. Defeat Stealmites until one of them drops a Teleport Key
7. Once you have the Teleport Key, return to the Import Assistant
8. To leave, enter the next room to the north (up)
9. Stand on the back wall against the teleporter
10. The teleporter will send you to Aeven North

   Tutorial
   Turkey
   Unlocked
   Import Completed

## Completion

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **Unknown**.
