**Location:** Aeven

**Level Requirement:** 1  
**Rewards:** 16,000 exp and 400 eons

Aevenomics-3 is a quest given by Aeven Manager in Aeven. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Aeven
   Aevenomics-3
2. The primary purpose of this quest line is for players to become familiar with many of the NPCs surrounding Aeven
3. Aevenomics-1 -> Aevenomics-2 -> Aevenomics-3

4. Go to the northern side of Aeven and talk to the Aeven Manager to start the quest
5. He tells you he has a final job opportunity and asks you to check on Babs Hair Salon, as well as both the Sea Port and Airport
6. First, head to Babs Hair Salon, located east of Aeven
7. At the salon, speak with Ivy Hair Products and receive her report
8. Return to the Aeven Manager and deliver Ivy’s report
9. Next, he sends you to Aeven Airdocks
10. Go north one map and take the elevator
11. Once on the higher level, head east to the Aeven Airdocks
12. Speak with Flynn Fly and discuss finances
13. Return to the Aeven Manager and deliver Flynn’s report
14. Then, the Aeven Manager now asks you to visit the Aeven Port
15. Travel south to the Aeven Port
16. Speak with the Sealine Merchant, located in the last house on the southeast side of the port
17. Discuss finances with the Sealine Merchant and receive his report
18. Return to the Aeven Manager with the final report

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **16,000 EXP, 400 Eons**.
