**Location:** Green Lake (Swamp)  
**Quest Giver:** Swamp Guard  
**Level Requirement:** 10  
**Rewards:** 8,400 exp and 500 eons

Guard Job is a quest given by Swamp Guard in Green Lake (Swamp). Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Aeven
   Guard Job
   Green Lake (Swamp)
   Swamp Guard
   Level 10
   8,400 exp and 500 eons
2. Go to the Green Lake map, located northeast of Aeven
3. Once there, talk to the Swamp Guard to start the quest
4. The Swamp Guard will explain that he is stationed there to guard the cave and ask you to help by killing 20 Swamp Spiders
5. Accept this request, then defeat 20 Swamp Spiders within the swamp
6. Collect 2 Spider Legs they drop, as you will need them to finish the quest
7. After defeating all 20 spiders, return to the Swamp Guard
8. He will require proof of the kills and ask you to hand over 2 Spider Legs
9. Give the Swamp Guard the 2 Spider Legs you collected
10. He will then instruct you to report to the Stronghold Chief, located west of the Green Lake map
