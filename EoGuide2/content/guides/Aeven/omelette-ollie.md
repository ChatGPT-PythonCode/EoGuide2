**Location:** Aeven (Fairy Soda Pub)  
**Quest Giver:** Omelette Ollie  
**Level Requirement:** Unknown  
**Rewards:** 10,000 exp and 600 eons

Omelette Ollie is a quest given by Omelette Ollie in Aeven (Fairy Soda Pub). Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Go to the Aeven Fairy Soda Pub and find Omelette Ollie to start the quest
2. He starts off by asking you to speak with the Bartender
3. The Bartender wants you to tell Omelette Ollie that omelets aren't on the menu
4. Omelette Ollie ask you to speak with the Bartender again
5. You end up telling the Bartender that you'll make the omelets
6. First head to the Aeven Market and but 30 Buckets and 10 Bottles from Cara Craft Merchant
7. Next head to Aeven Farm Outlet by going 1 map west of Aeven
8. Milk Cows until you have 30 Buckets of Milk
9. After you click on the Cow you can right click the Empty Bucket in your inventory to put it in the slot to fill with milk
10. After you have 30 Buckets of Milk go into the house next to the Cow Sign
11. In the back click on the Milk Bottle Station
12. Craft 10 Bottles of Milk
13. The last step is getting the Eggs
14. Go to the Aeven Market and collect eggs until you have 6 of them
15. Once you have 6 Eggs speak with Ramdon Goresy on the eastern side of the market
16. Craft two Omelette Plates
   Aeven
   Turkey
   Tower Patrol
   Man Hungry

## Completion

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **10,000 EXP, 600 Eons**.
