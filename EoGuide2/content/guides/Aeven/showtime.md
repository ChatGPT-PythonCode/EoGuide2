**Location:** Aeven Theatre  
**Quest Giver:** Aeven Magician  
**Level Requirement:** 1  
**Rewards:** 2000 exp

Showtime is a quest given by Aeven Magician in Aeven Theatre. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Go to the Aeven Theatre and talk to the Aeven Magician to start the quest
2. (Aeven Theatre is where the Bank used to be)
   He has a lot of dialog, click through it until he asks you to meet him on the Main Stage
3. Go through the doors east of the Aeven Magician
4. He will ask you to follow him to the stage
5. Talk to him again and he will ask you to be his assistant
6. The Aeven Magician's first request is to go to the backstage and search the chest for his Bunny
7. Return to him and tell him you can't find the Bunny
   His next request is for you to go to the backstage and go through the floor-hatch
8. Return to the Aeven Magician and tell him the bad news
9. After the crowd leaves talk to the Aeven Manager again
10. He'll ask you to meet him in the Main Lobby again
