**Rewards:** 200 exp

Training is a quest given by Trainer Steve in Aeven North Entrance. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Go to the Aeven North Entrance map (north of Aeven) and talk to Trainer Steve
2. Trainer Steve explains how to engage in combat and has you practice first on the Beginner Dummy
3. Defeat the Beginner Dummy on the south side of the house
4. After its defeated a in-game message will appear telling you to talk to Trainer Steve
5. Trainer Steve will then task you with defeating the Novice Dummy
6. Defeat the Novice Dummy on the south side of the house
7. After its defeated a in-game message will appear telling you to talk to Trainer Steve
