Toy Repair is a quest given by Little Eddy in Aeven. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

**What you'll need:** 4 Toy Car Wheels

1. Go to Aeven Square and find Little Eddy to start the Quest
2. Little Eddy will ask you to help him fix his Toy Car by finding 4 Toy Car Wheels
3. Toy Car Wheels drop from Crows and Rats around Aeven
4. Defeating Crows in Aeven North might be the best option since there are a lot of Crows
5. Once you collected 4 Toy Car Wheels Head to the Aeven Forge
6. Once there speak with Metal Mia and craft the Toy Car
7. Return to Little Eddy
8. He will give you a new Frame since the game thinks you lost the last one
9. Click I take it
