**Location:** Aeven Outlet (Piggy Farm)  
**Quest Giver:** Billy Farmhand  
**Level Requirement:** 1  
**Rewards:** 600 exp and 1 Buddy Coin

Runaway Piggy is a quest given by Billy Farmhand in Aeven Outlet (Piggy Farm). Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Head East of Aeven 1 Map and find Billy Farmhand at the Piggy Farm
2. He will ask you to find his Runaway Piggy, the piggy can be found wandering randomly around the same map
3. Once you find the Runaway Piggy, walk up to him and click on him to pick him up
4. Return to Billy Farmhand to claim your 600 exp and 1 Buddy Coin
