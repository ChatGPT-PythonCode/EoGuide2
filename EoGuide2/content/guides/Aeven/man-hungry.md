**Rewards:** 8,000 exp and 280 eons

Man Hungry is a quest given by Hungry Man in Aeven (Fairy Soda Pub). Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Aeven
   Man Hungry
   Aeven (Fairy Soda Pub)
   Hungry Man
   8,000 exp and 280 eons
2. Go to Aeven Fairy Soda Pub and find the Hungry Man to start the quest
3. He will ask you to make him a Bacon Egg Plate, agree and finish the dialog
4. Leave the pub and go to the Aeven Market and speak with Ramdon Goresy to make the Bacon Egg Plates
5. Next go east to the Piggy Farm and collect 7 Bacon by defeating Piggies
6. Between the Aeven and the Piggy Farm you will find a patch with Carrot and Crop Salad; you need to collect 3 Crop Salads
7. After collecting 3 Eggs, 7 Bacon, and 3 Crop Salads return to Ramdon Gorsey in the Aeven Market
8. Craft the Bacon Egg Plate, once you have it go back to the Hungry Man in the Aeven Pub
   Aeven
   Omelette Ollie

## Completion

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **8,000 EXP, 280 Eons**.
