**Location:** Aeven West

**Level Requirement:** 1  
**Rewards:** 680 exp and 20 eons

Boots For Dinner (rep) is a quest given by Dude Without Boots in Aeven West. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Aeven
   Boots For Dinner (rep)
   Aeven West
   Dude Without Boots
   Level 1
   680 exp and 20 eons
   Yes (up to level 14)
2. Go to Aeven West and speak with Dude Without Boots
3. He will tell you that his boots have been eaten by goats and ask you to retrieve them for him
4. Walk the map killing goats until a pair of Eaten Boots drops
5. (Drop Rate for Eaten Boots is 1%)
   Aeven
   Sky Unclogged
   Broken Gear

## Completion

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **680 EXP, 20 Eons**.
