Funky Hair is a quest given by Hair Guy in Aeven Hair Salon. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Aeven
   Funky Hair
   Aeven Hair Salon
   Hair Guy
   8,200 exp and 200 eons
   10 Sticky Slime
2. Go to the Aeven Hair Salon, located west of Aeven just past the Witch Shop
3. Once inside, talk to Hair Guy to start the quest
4. On the final dialog window, click Okay
5. Unless you already have 10 Sticky Slimes then click Got it Here!
   After accepting the quest, head northwest to the Aeven Blobsie Gardens
6. Defeat Blobsies until you collect 10 Sticky Slime
7. (Drop rate: 4%)
   Once you have all 10 Sticky Slimes, return to Hair Guy at the Aeven Hair Salon
   Aeven
   Turkey
   Swaggy Santa
   Santa Transport (rep)
   Santa SOS (rep)

## Completion

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **8,200 EXP, 200 Eons**.
