**Location:** Shadow Desert (Orcy's Room)  
**Quest Giver:** Baby Orcy  
**Level Requirement:** Unknown  
**Rewards:** 6,900 exp and Self Heal Spell

Orcy's Secret 1 is a quest given by Baby Orcy in Shadow Desert (Orcy's Room). Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Aeven
   Orcy's Secret 1
   Shadow Desert (Orcy's Room)
   Baby Orcy
   6,900 exp and Self Heal Spell
   Shrink Key (1 Minor Potion Bottle, 4 Green Berries, 15 Carrots, 2 Snake Rattles & 60 eons)
2. First you need to craft the Shrink Key at the Aeven Witch, You will need: 1 Minor Potion Bottle, 4 Green Berries, 15 Carrots, 2 Snake Rattles & 60 eons
3. After you have made the Shrink Key go to the Shadow Desert
4. Go through Aeven Mines till you reach the cart tracks and follow them out of the cave to the east
5. Walk east until you see a tiny door on the side of a hill
6. Go in the door and speak with Baby Orcy, just click next and ok
7. You will receive Orcys Pacy
8. Now leave the Desert going back West into the Mines, once in the mine go north and follow the wall around to a exit on the north wall
9. Once you come out of the Mines, go 1 more map up and then you will be at Aeven Falls
10. Continue north over the bridges and between the waterfalls
11. Go inside the small cave and walk up to the altar and click on it
