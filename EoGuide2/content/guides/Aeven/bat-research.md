Bat Research is a quest given by Dr in Aeven North Entrance. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Aeven
   Bat Research
   Aeven North Entrance
   Dr
2. Flapwell
   2,500 exp, 120 eons
3. Go to the Aeven North Entrance map, north of Aeven, and talk to Dr
4. Flapwell is researching the wings of Small Bats and ask you to assist him by bringing him 5 Bat Wings
5. Once you accept the quest, enter the Aeven Mines to the north and defeat Small Bats until you collect 5 Bat Wings
6. (Bat Wings has a 10% drop rate from Small Bats)
7. Return to Dr
