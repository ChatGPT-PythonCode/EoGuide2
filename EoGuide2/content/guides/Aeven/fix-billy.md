**Location:** Aeven (Town Square)  
**Quest Giver:** Billy Broke  
**Level Requirement:** 3  
**Rewards:** 25,000 exp

Fix Billy is a quest given by Billy Broke in Aeven (Town Square). Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Aeven
   Fix Billy
   Aeven (Town Square)
   Billy Broke
   25,000 exp
   Note:
   The quest
   Lost Wallet
   (minimum level 3) is a prerequisite to start this quest
2. Talk to Broke Billy in the Aeven Town Square to start the quest
3. You tell Broke Billy he has a hole in his pocket
4. He’s worried about the cost of new pants  so you tell him you’ll talk to Creative Caroline on his behalf
5. Head east and enter the Aeven Market and find creative Caroline
6. Creative Caroline tells you to inform Broke Billy that she will repair his pockets at no cost
7. Return to Broke Billy in the Aeven Town Square and he’ll thank you but then mention he lost a childhood photo and asks you to help him find it
8. After talking with him immediately do
   #Range
9. You should see where the Childhood Photo is
10. (If it's not popping up, move around Aeven doing
   #Range
   until it does)
11. Go to the coordinates provided and find/ pickup the Childhood Photo
   Aeven
   Turkey
   Lost Wallet
   Power Repo

## Completion

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **Unknown**.
