**Location:** Aeven (Blobsie Garden)  
**Quest Giver:** Florabella  
**Level Requirement:** Unknown  
**Rewards:** 12,600 exp and 800 eons

Flora Collector is a quest given by Florabella in Aeven (Blobsie Garden). Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. 1.Go to the Blobsie Garden
2. Continue all the way north (up) until you find Florabella
3. She needs rare flowers to finish her flower cave
4. First, she asks for Star Flowers, which can be found in Tropica
5. Go to Tropica and gather 10 star flowers
6. There are different spawns at many different areas around Tropica
7. Or, you can buy the flowers from other players
8. Give the 10 star flowers to Florabella
9. She then asks you for 10 Sky Flowers, which can be found in Sky World
10. Go to Sky World and gather 10 sky flowers
11. There are different spawns at many different areas around Sky World
12. Or, you can buy the flowers from other players
13. Give the 10 sky flowers to Florabella
14. After, she asks you for 10 Ice Flowers, which can be found within the Ice Cave in Slowlandia
15. Gather the 10 ice flowers at the Ice Cave
16. There are different spawns within almost every room
17. If you don't have your warmth gear with you, you'll lose some health over time
18. Or, you can buy the flowers from other players
   Orcy's Secret 2

## Completion

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **12,600 EXP, 800 Eons**.
