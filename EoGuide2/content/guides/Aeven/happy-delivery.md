**Location:** Aeven North Entrance  
**Quest Giver:** Happy Berry Picker  
**Level Requirement:** 1  
**Rewards:** 320 exp and 70 eons

Happy Delivery is a quest given by Happy Berry Picker in Aeven North Entrance. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Aeven
   Happy Delivery
   Aeven North Entrance
   Happy Berry Picker
   320 exp and 70 eons
   Note:
   The quest
   Happy Berries
   (minimum level 1) is a prerequisite to start this quest
2. Go to the Aeven North Entrance map (north of Aeven) and talk to the Happy Berry Picker
3. She thanks you for helping her earlier and asks you to deliver a batch of harvested berries to either Happy Berry Mike or Happy Berry Jane
4. Go south, then slightly east, and find Happy Berry Mike or Happy Berry Jane at the Aeven Market
5. Happy Berry Mike will thank you for delivering the berries and give you 50 eons
