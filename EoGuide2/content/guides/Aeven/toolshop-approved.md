Toolshop Approved is a quest. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Aeven
   Toolshop Approved
   Starting Point: Aeven
   Repeatable: No
   Start Level: 1
   Max Level: NONE
   EXP: 8,200
   EON: 0
2. Speak with the Shady Doorkeeper
3. He can be found behind the church in Aeven
4. He will ask for a small fee of 50 eons, Agree
5. He will tell you to speak with the Woodcutter to the east of the Mine Entrance
6. Leave Aeven going back to the Mine Entrance where you first spawn into Aeven and then go East
7. Once you have reached the next map (Green Lake) you will see the wood cutter sitting beside a fire
8. Speak with the wood cutter and he will ask you to bring him 10 wood logs
9. Then, he will give you a Axe
10. The item is called Old Choppy
11. Equip the axe and get to work! You can find trees scatterned around aeven
12. There are two on the green lake map and 5 on the previous map with Mine entrance
13. You can find farmable trees by hovering your mouse over them, once you have located one walk up to it with your axe equipped and hold down the CTRL key untill the tree has been chopped and turned into a stump
14. Once you have farmed 10 log, return to the Woodcutter and turn them in
15. He will thank you for the wood and then ask you to speak with the Deep Miner inside the Deep Mines
16. Leave the map going back West to the Mine Entrance then go north into the mines
17. Once your in the mines continue north until you reach a cave door on next to tracks and the Bat Smash Quest
18. Go inside and speak with the Deep Miner
19. She will ask you to farm 10 ore for her and give you a mine pick
20. The item is called Rusty Diggy
21. Leave the room and go back into the mine, there are multiple deposits of Ore thought-out the map walk up to them with the mine pick equipped and hold down the CTRL key until you have mined the deposit and have the Ore in your inventory
22. Once you have collected all 10  Ore return to the Deep Miner and turn them in
23. She will thank you for your work and tell you to speak with shady
24. Return to Aeven and speak with the Shady Doorkeeper to finish the quest!
