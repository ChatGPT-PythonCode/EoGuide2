Making Buddies (Daily) is a quest. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Aeven
   Making Buddies (Daily)
2. Start the quest in your Quest tab and head to Nostalgia Island
3. Be sure you have at least 2 eggs with you!
4. Once you get off the boat go a south unto the next map, once there go to the house at the bottom left corner
5. Enter the house and speak with Buddy Chef to make your Buddy food
6. You will need 5 Chewy Herb and 1 Egg per food, and you need 2 Buddy food to complete the quest
7. Once you have your 2 Buddy food, head back to aeven and go east to the Outlet (Map with piggy farm)
8. Go inside and speak with Britney to trade your two buddy food for your Buddy Coin!
