Flutter Friends (Weekly) is a quest. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Aeven
   Flutter Friends (Weekly)
   A new buddy can be found at Blobsie Gardens,
   this weekly quest refreshes every Monday at server reset (3 AM EST)
2. Speak to the Florabella NPC, and click on the workbench behind her to craft the required items
3. Florabella will request 24 bouquets of one random type: Aeven, Starflower, Skyflower, or Ice Flower
4. Aeven Bouquet
   - Each requires 2 Aevenflower and 3 Puffyweed
5. For 24 bouquets, you’ll need 48 Aevenflower and 72 Puffyweed
6. The new gatherable Aevenflower can be found at: (turn on your minimap to help you find these exact coordinates):
   Aeven North (1 map north from aeven mines cave) (map id 76) at x: 43 y:8 and at x:16 y:2
   Aeven Falls Grotto (northwest of Aeven mines cave) (map id 78) at x: 56 y:38 and at
   x: 35 y: 54
   Aeven Farmlet (West of Aeven) (map id 43), x:37 y:50 and x:12 y:58
   Aeven Castle Garden (West of Aeven farmlet) (map id 17), x:16 y:56 and at x:28 y:19
   Aeven Railroads (map id 44) x:36 y:62, at x: 7 y: 72, and at x: 25 y: 37
   Butterfly Plains (near Anundo, map outside of Cyclops Cave) (map id 197) at x:43 y:12, at x: 63 y: 20, and at x:34 y:37
   Star Flower Bouquet
   - Each requires 2 Starflower and 3 Puffyweed
7. For 24 bouquets, you’ll need 48 Starflowers (found in Tropica) and 72 Puffyweed
8. Sky Flower Bouquet
   - Each requires 2 Skyflower and 3 Chewyherb
9. For 24 bouquets, you’ll need 48 Skyflowers (found in Skyworld) and 72 Chewyherb (found in Nostalgia)
10. Ice Flower Bouquet
   - Each requires 2 Ice Flowers and 3 Chewyherb
11. For 24 bouquets, you’ll need 48 Ice Flowers (found in Snowlandia’s Ice Cave) and 72 Chewyherb (found in Nostalgia)
