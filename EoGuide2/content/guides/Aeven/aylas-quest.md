**Location:** Aeven  
**Quest Giver:** Ayla  
**Level Requirement:** 3  
**Rewards:** 2,200 exp and 100 eons

Ayla's Quest is a quest given by Ayla in Aeven. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Go to the Aeven Witch Shop, located on the east side of Aeven
2. Talk to Ayla, who is
   sitting outside, to start the quest
3. Ayla explains she is trying to join the Order of the Witches, but she forgot the final ingredient for her magic brew
4. She suggests you pose as a witch and speak to the Witch inside the shop
5. Enter the Aeven Witch Shop and talk to the Witch
   During the dialogue, select the following options in order:
   ·       Hello fellow witch
6. ·       I studied witchcraft for years
7. ·       What is the missing ingredient?
   ·       Yes, I knew that all along
8. Exit the shop and speak with Ayla again
9. She will explain that Moonpetals blossom at Aeven Top
10. Head to Aeven Top by going to the Aeven Top Lift, north of Aeven
11. Take the next lift just north of you
12. Collect the Moonpetal from the ground
13. Return to Ayla outside the Witch Shop in Aeven
