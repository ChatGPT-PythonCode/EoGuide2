**Location:** Aeven (Market)  
**Quest Giver:** Goat Collector  
**Level Requirement:** 1  
**Rewards:** 920 exp and 34 eons

Goat Horns (rep) is a quest given by Goat Collector in Aeven (Market). Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

**What you'll need:** 6 Goat Horns

1. Speak with the Goat Collector in the Aeven Market
2. He will ask you to help him by collecting and bringing him 6 Goat Horns
3. Defeat goats in the Aeven Market, Aeven North or Aeven West until you collect 6 Goat Horns
4. Once you gathered 6 Goat Horns return to the Goat Collector and claim your prize of 920 exp and 34 eons
