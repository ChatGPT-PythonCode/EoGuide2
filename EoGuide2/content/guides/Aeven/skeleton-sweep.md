**Location:** Aeven Entombment

**Level Requirement:** Unknown  
**Rewards:** 4,000 exp and 400 eons

Skeleton Sweep is a quest given by Royal Scout in Aeven Entombment. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Head to the Aeven Entombment (South of the Castle) and find the Royal Scout
2. Speak with the Royal Scout who's outside of the Aeven Catacombs
3. He will ask you to defeat 20 Skeletons within the Catacombs
4. Enter the Aeven Catacombs and defeat 20 Skeletons
5. All the Skeletons count towards the kill count

   Turkey

   Man Hungry

## Completion

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **4,000 EXP, 400 Eons**.
