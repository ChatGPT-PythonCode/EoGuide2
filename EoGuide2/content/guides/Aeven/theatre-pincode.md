**Location:** Aeven Theatre  
**Quest Giver:** Aeven Magician  
**Level Requirement:** 1  
**Rewards:** 1,200 exp and 100 eons

Theatre Pincode is a quest given by Aeven Magician in Aeven Theatre. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Aeven
   Theatre Pincode
   Aeven Theatre
   Aeven Magician
   Level 1
   1,200 exp and 100 eons
   Note:
   The quest
   Showtime
   is a prerequisite to start this quest
2. Go to the Aeven Theatre and talk to the Aeven Magician to start the quest
3. (Aeven Theatre is where the Bank used to be)
   The Aeven Magician wants you to look around the theatre and investigate
4. Go north of the Aeven Magician and speak to the Chicken CC
5. Select the Honest? option
6. Select the You Seem Nervous Option
7. A in-game window will pop up informing you to spy from a distance
8. Follow the Chicken CC and watch him enter a code and enter the northern door
9. Walk up to the door and another in-game window will pop up
10. Enter the northeast door
11. Grid 18,8
   Speak to Chicken CC again
   Aeven
   Turkey
   Showtime

## Completion

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **1,200 EXP, 100 Eons**.
