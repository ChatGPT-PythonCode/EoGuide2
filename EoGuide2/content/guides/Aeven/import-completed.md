**Location:** Bunker 5 (Shadow Desert)

**Rewards:** 200 exp and Peasant Cloths

Import Completed is a quest given by Inventor in Bunker 5 (Shadow Desert). Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Aeven
   Import Completed
   Bunker 5 (Shadow Desert)
   Inventor
   Level 0
   200 exp and Peasant Cloths
2. Your journey begins on the Import Pad, once your character has been loaded go West two maps
3. Once you have reached the room with the Inventor and Assistant click on the inventor to open dialog
4. Once you have finished the dialog with the Inventor, he will tell you to speak to his Assistant
5. Speak with the Import Assistant and he will give you a set of Peasant Clothes
6. For a basic ingame tutorial select No
7. Otherwise select Yes and complete the quest
   Aeven
   Tutorial
   Unlocked
   Getting Ready

## Completion

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **Unknown**.
