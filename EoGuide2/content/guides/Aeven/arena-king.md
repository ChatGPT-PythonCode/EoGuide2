**Location:** Aeven Castle Garden  
**Quest Giver:** Arena Doorkeeper  
**Level Requirement:** Unknown  
**Rewards:** 200,000 exp and 1,000 eons

Arena King is a quest given by Arena Doorkeeper in Aeven Castle Garden. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Head to the Arena, either by walking from Aeven or clicking on the social games tab on the left side of your game
2. Talk to the Arena Doorkeeper
3. He asks you to play 100 matches
4. Head inside and participate in 100 arena matches! You can track your progress on your Q tab at the top left of your game
5. After completing this quest, it turns into a weekly quest that you can complete 1x a week for 40,000 exp and 500 eons

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **200,000 EXP, 1,000 Eons**.
