**Location:** Army Tent  
**Quest Giver:** Field Medic  
**Level Requirement:** Unknown  
**Rewards:** 100,000 exp and 1,440 eons

Critical Aid is a quest given by Field Medic in Army Tent. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. 1.At the Aeven Battleground east of Stronghold, go to the tent at coordinates 12, 47
2. Talk to the medic inside
3. 2.He tells you to bring a medikit from a different tent
4. Go to the upper leftmost tent (the one on the left of the Soldier Captain NPC) and grab the medikits
5. 3.The medikit stockpile is empty, so go back to the medic
6. 4.The medic asks you to make a medkit
7. To craft them, go to the Aeven Farmlet map (milk farm)
8. It is 1 map west of Aeven where the cows are
9. Go inside the house at coordinates 35, 12
10. Medikits are crafted with 10 craft coin+500 eon, 10 hunter coin+200 eon, 12 ancient coin+400 eon, or 12 shiny coin+400 eon
11. After this quest is done, talk to the medic again to start the next quest - Medikit mystery

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **100,000 EXP, 1,440 Eons**.
