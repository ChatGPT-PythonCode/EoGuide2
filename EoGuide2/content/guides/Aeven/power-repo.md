Power Repo is a quest. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Aeven
   Power Repo
2. Talk to Little Eddy in Aeven Square - he lost his toy car again
3. You’ll find the car north of Aeven close to the potion store, Take it to Little Eddy, he says the battery is missing
4. He claims someone must’ve seen something strange
5. Talk to Happy Berry Picker north of Aeven, she will tell you that she saw a little mountain dwelling gnome with a battery and hid his entrance key in a bush beside a little door
6. Walk to the north east corner of the map, here you will find the Small Door and to the right of it youll see a bush with the key
7. (Key can be found hidden at x46 - y11)
8. Go inside that door and speak to the gnome, choose to take the battery the run!
