**Location:** Aeven Entombment  
**Quest Giver:** Royal Scout  
**Level Requirement:** 30  
**Rewards:** 2,000 exp and 1 Royal Writs

Royal Writs (daily) is a quest given by Royal Scout in Aeven Entombment. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Aeven
   Royal Writs (daily)
   Aeven Entombment
   Royal Scout
   Level 30
   2,000 exp and 1 Royal Writs
   Note:
   This is the only way to acquire Royal Writs
2. They are used for crafting Royal Arch Armor which requires 40 Royal Writs
3. The quest
   Skeleton Sweep
   is a prerequisite to start this quest
   Head to the Aeven Entombment and find the Royal Scout
4. The Royal Scout will ask you to defeat 10 Grave Archers within the Aeven Catacombs
5. Enter the Aeven Catacombs and defeat 10 Grave Archers
6. Go North 2 Maps and enter the Aeven Castle and speak with the Royal Writkeeper to craft your Royal Arch Armor
