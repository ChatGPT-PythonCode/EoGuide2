**Location:** Aeven Port  
**Quest Giver:** Port Master  
**Level Requirement:** Unknown  
**Rewards:** 10,000 exp and 500 eons

Sealiner Repairs (daily) is a quest given by Port Master in Aeven Port. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Go to the Aeven Port and talk to the Port Master to start this quest
2. He’ll ask you to bring him 10 Wood Logs
3. If you don’t already have them, head north then east and gather 10 Wood Logs by chopping down trees
