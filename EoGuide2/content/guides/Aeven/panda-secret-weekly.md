Panda Secret (weekly) is a quest given by Peeka Panda in Panda Place. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

   If you know how to get to the Panda Place skip to Step 7
2. Otherwise, you can follow the video or visual guide below
3. From Aeven, travel north into the Aeven Mines
4. Take the northern exit inside the Aeven Mines
5. Go halfway up Aeven North, then head west into the Aeven Grotto
6. Once inside the Grotto, cross the northern bridge and go behind the waterfall
7. At the end, you’ll find a secret exit
8. Head west and talk to Panda Express, then select
   “
   Take me there.”
   You’ll arrive at Panda Place
9. Head northwest until you find Peeka Panda
10. Speak to Peeka Panda and pay him 500 eons
11. He will take you to the Panda Secret Chest
12. Open the chest for a chance to obtain the Panda Costume
