**Location:** Fairy Soda Pub  
**Quest Giver:** Hungry Man  
**Level Requirement:** 8  
**Rewards:** 1,280 exp and 110 eons

Still Hungry (rep) is a quest given by Hungry Man in Fairy Soda Pub. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

   1 Bacon Egg Plate (Materials: 3 Eggs, 3 Crop Salad and 7 Bacon)
2. Head to the Aeven Fairy Soda Pub, and find Hungry Man
3. Speak to Hungry Man
4. He’ll ask you to bring him a Bacon Egg Plate
5. Leave the pub and head northeast to the Aeven Market
6. Collect 3 Eggs from the Chicken Nesting Boxes
7. After collecting the Eggs, go east of Aeven and stop by the Crop Salad patch
8. Harvest any Crop Salad that has spawned
9. Continue east until you reach the Piggy Farm
10. Defeat Piglets or the Mother Piglet until you collect 7 Bacon
11. ·      Bacon has a 10% drop rate from both Piglets and Mother Piglet
12. If you still need more Crop Salad, check the patch again on your way back west
13. If none are available, wait at the patch until they respawn
14. Once you have all 3 Crop Salad, head back to the Aeven Market and find Ramdon Goresy in the northeast corner
15. Talk to Ramdon Goresy and craft 1 Bacon Egg Plate
