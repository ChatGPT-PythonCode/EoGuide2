Milk Run is a quest given by Milkman Mike in Aeven Farmlet. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Go to the Aeven Farmlet map, west of Aeven, and talk to Milkman Mike
2. Milkman Mike explains he is running out of bottles of milk for his next delivery and asks for your help
3. To craft
   3 Bottles of Milk
   , first head east into Aeven and buy
   9 Buckets
   and
   3 Empty Bottles
   from
   Cara Craft Merchant
   in the Aeven Market
4. Return to the
   Aeven Farmlet
   and find a cow
5. To milk a cow:
   ·      Stand next to it and click on it
6. ·      A window will appear
7. Drag an empty bucket into the left box to fill it with milk
8. ·      Once full, click on the bucket to collect it
9. ·      Repeat until you have
   9 Buckets of Milk
   Go into the house next to
   Milkman Mike
   to craft the
   Bottles of Milk
10. Inside, head to the back of the room where you’ll find
   Ronald Milk
   and the
   Milk Bottle Stations
11. Use one of the
   Milk Bottle Stations
   to craft
   3 Bottles of Milk
12. Return to
   Milkman Mike
   outside the house
13. Give him the
   3 Bottles of Milk
   1,700 EXP
   and
   300 eons
