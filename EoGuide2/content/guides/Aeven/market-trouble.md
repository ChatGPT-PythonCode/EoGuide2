**Location:** Aeven  
**Quest Giver:** Marna Milk  
**Level Requirement:** 1  
**Rewards:** 700 exp and 40 eons

Market Trouble is a quest given by Marna Milk in Aeven. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Go to the north of Aeven
2. Once there, talk to Marna Milk to start the quest
3. She will explain that she was kicked out of the market and ask you to speak with Ronald Milk inside the Milk Farm
4. Go to the Milk Farm and talk to Ronald Milk
5. Ronald is shocked to learn that Marna is kicked out of the market
6. He asks you to let her know that he will speak to the manager on her behalf
7. Return to Marna Milk
