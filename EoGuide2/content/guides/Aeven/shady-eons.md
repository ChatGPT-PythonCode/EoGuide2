**Location:** Aeven  
**Quest Giver:** Shady Doorkeeper  
**Level Requirement:** 6  
**Rewards:** 10,000 exp and 800 eons

## Walkthrough

1. Aeven
   Aeven
   Shady Doorkeeper
   Level 6
   10,000 exp and 800 eons
2. Speak with the Shady Doorkeeper outside the Aeven Woodshop
3. He will ask if you're interested in a special job
4. Select "Yes!" & "Yes!" then he will ask you to speak with the Port Master
5. Go to Aeven Port and continue south until you reach the Port Master, he is next to the last ticket shop on the bottom right side of map
6. Speak with him and select "Yep, Here for buiness" & "We have to settle this"
7. When your done with the dialog with the Port Master return to shady to give him the news
8. He will now ask you go to Stronghold and speak with the Stronghold chief
9. Now go to Stronghold and enter the building in the north west corner of the map and speak with the chief
10. Choose "Shady is not a thief.", "Shady is the best guy i know." & "Shady doesn't like carrots"
11. Return to Shady and give him the news
12. He will now ask you to go to Tropica Island and speak with Coco Jen
13. Go to Aeven Port and take SeaLiner 101 to Tropica Port
14. Once you get off the boat in Tropica follow the beach to the East and then onto the next map
15. On the next map take the road to the North leading into a village
16. Once in the village go to the far west ide of the map (The last house on the left) and enter it
17. Once inside speak with Coco Jen and choose: "hear me out", "He did not mean it" & "Your toilet shines!"
18. After you're done speaking with Coco Jen return to Shady and give him the news
19. He will now ask you to go to Skywonder and speak with Bank Mommy
20. Take Airliner 121 from Aeven to Skywonder
21. Once off the ship in Skywonder, follow the road north and then east
22. Once you are on the next map follow the road north until the splits then go West into the City of Skywonder and you will see the bank on the start of the map
23. Go inside the bank and speak with Bank Mommy choose: "I'm here no behalf of Shady" & "Yes, Please cancel"
24. Aeven
   Tutorial
   Swaggy Santa
   Santa Transport (rep)
   Santa SOS (rep)

## Completion

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **10,000 EXP, 800 Eons**.
