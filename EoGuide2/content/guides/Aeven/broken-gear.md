**Location:** Aeven North  
**Quest Giver:** Fallen Warrior  
**Level Requirement:** 1  
**Rewards:** 400 exp, 1 Broken Dagger and 1 Broken Shield

Broken Gear is a quest given by Fallen Warrior in Aeven North. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Level 1 (however you need to be level 3 to enter the Aeven Mines)
   400 exp, 1 Broken Dagger and 1 Broken Shield
2. Speak with the Fallen Warrior outside of the Aeven Mine Cave
3. He will tell you he lost his Amulet between Bunker 1 and 2 in the Shadow Desert
4. Go into Aeven Mines and stay along the East wall once you reach the mine cart tracks follow the outside of the cave into the Shadow Desert
5. Once you're in the Shadow Desert go north past the Wayfairers into the next map
6. Now continue North until you see Bunker 2 and Bunker 1 you will find the amulet between the two
7. Pick it up and return to the Fallen Warrior
