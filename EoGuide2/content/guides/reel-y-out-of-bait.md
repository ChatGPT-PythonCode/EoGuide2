2. If not, go to the Green Lake map and fish until you have 20 Silver Fish
3. Or you can buy them from other players
4. Once you have the fish, head west to the Aeven Railroads and board the train to Hallodale
5. After leaving the train, travel east into Hallodale, then south to reach the Hallodale Docks
6. Speak to Baitfisher Ben on the docks to start the quest
7. He will ask you for 20 silver fish
8. Give him the 20 Silver Fish, and he will reward you with 100,000 exp and 800 eons
