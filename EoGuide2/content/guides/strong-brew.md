## Walkthrough

Location:
Centaur Wine Farm
Quest Giver:
Centaur Queen
Level Requirement:
Level 15
Reward:
42,000 exp and 300 eons
WHAT YOU NEED:
30 gatherable brew and 10 horseshoes
Go to the Aeven Port and take Sealiner 106 to Centaur Port
2. Keep going north until you reach Centaur Village and enter the Centaur Wine Farm
3. Once inside
speak to the Centaur Queen
4. She will ask you to gather 30 Brews from the cellar
5. Go north and into the basement
6. Retrieve 30 Gatherable Brew from the cellar
7. There are 6 spots scattered throughout the map
8. Once you collected 30 return to the Centaur Queen
9. She will prompt you to go see Centaur Cita
10. Leave the house and go outside
11. Go west to the next map, and Centaur Cita is in the very first house
12. She will give you a brew to put in the cellar wine rack
13. Go back to the wine cellar map where you gathered the 30 brews and walk all the way north
14. By walking up against the wall, it will place the wine on the cellar
15. Once you get the pop-up, return to the Centaur Queen
16. Return to the Centaur Queen, she will prompt you to collect 10 Horseshoes for her guards
17. Defeating the regular Centaur outside of the Centaur Queens house would be the safe and one of the fastest ways to acquire these
18. Once you have collected 10 Horseshoes, return to the Centaur Queen and receive your reward of 42,000 exp and 300 eon
