Carrot HelpLocation: Aeven OutletQuest Giver: Carrot GirlLevel Requirement:  1WHAT YOU'LL NEED: 3 CarrotsGo to the Aeven Outlet, located on the east side of Aeven.  On the west side of the pig pen, talk to Carrot Girl to start the quest.Carrot Girl explains that she can only find Stinky Carrots and asks you to bring her 3 normal Carrots.Head west across the road where you’ll find 2 Carrot Patches. Collect 3 Carrots from them.Return to Carrot Girl and give her the 3 normal Carrots.  She will thank you and reward you with 500 exp and 60 eons.
