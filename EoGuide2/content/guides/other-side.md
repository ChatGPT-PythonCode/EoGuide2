Other Side is a quest that tasks you with assisting Unknown in Haunted Hallows. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Haunted Hallows
Other Side
WHAT YOU NEED:
3 iron, 6 springs, 1 love potion
2. Go to the south of the port and take Aeven Sealiner 104 to Haunted Hallows
3. After arriving at the Haunted Hallows port, go 1 map north
4. On the east side of the map is the Haunted House
5. Go inside, and head west down the stairs
6. After, head south
7. Follow the rug and it will lead you to the Lost Soul
8. Speak with The Lost Soul and he says some nonsense
9. Go to the inventor
10. This is located past the cave north of Aeven in Bunker 5
11. He will ask you for 3 iron and 6 springs to make a ghost box
12. The inventor will then give you a ghost box
13. Go back to the Lost Soul npc in the basement of the Haunted House and double click on the ghost box when you are in front of the npc
14. He longs to see the face of Marie in a photograph in order to move on
15. Go to the picnic spot in Aeven Top and pick it up from the ground
16. Take the Lost Photograph back to the Haunted House Lost Soul
17. Double click on the photo in your inventory when you are in front of the npc
18. Craft a potion of love at the witch using an empty minor potion from the pot store next to the Aeven bank, 2 puffy weed, 3 purple berries and 3 pink berries
19. Go back to the Lost Soul and use the love potion it in front of it
20. Then, you will be rewarded with 82,000 xp
21. Haunted Hallows
One-Time Quest

## Completion

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **Unknown**.
