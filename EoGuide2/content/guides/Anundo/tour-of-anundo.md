**Location:** Anundo  
**Quest Giver:** Yupi Juniper  
**Level Requirement:** 3  
**Rewards:** 6,000 exp

Tour of Anundo is a quest given by Yupi Juniper in Anundo. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Travel to Anundo
2. You can follow the video below or keep scrolling for the visual tutorial
3. If starting in Aeven follow these steps:
   ·      Go north to enter Aeven North Entrance
4. ·      Before reaching the Aeven Mines, head east into Green Lake
5. ·      Travel north through the Swamp
6. ·      Take the lift
7. Tip:
   Dash is highly recommended for this section
8. You can purchase it from the Skills Wizard in Aeven when you reach level 3
9. After the lift, head north into the next map
10. (Beware: The Hyper Carnivor is extremely fast and deals heavy damage
11. Move west to the stairway, avoiding the Hyper Carnivor, and enter the stairs
12. Solve the staircase puzzle by following this path:
   South  North  Southeast
13. Here, go south
14. Here, go north
15. Here, go southeast
16. Continue north to enter Anundo
17. Use the map for reference points within Anundo
18. Talk to Yupi Juniper
19. She begins your tour by asking you to visit Panda Kopan first
20. Head northwest to reach Panda Kopan’s cottage
21. Talk to Panda Kopan
22. Return to Yupi Juniper
23. She will then send you to visit Banker Maya
24. Head north and enter the Anundo Bank
25. Banker Maya will be behind the counter
26. Banker Maya reassures you that your eons are safe with her
27. After the dialogue, return to Yupi Juniper
28. Yupi Juniper’s final stop is the Anundo Waterfalls
29. Go east, cross the bridge, and then head north until an in-game message appears
   Anundo
   Tutorial
   Turkey
   Destiny (rep)
   Special Tea

## Completion

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **Unknown**.
