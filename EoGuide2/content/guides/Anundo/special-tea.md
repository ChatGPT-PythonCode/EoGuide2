**Location:** Anundo  
**Quest Giver:** Old Hermit  
**Level Requirement:** 20  
**Rewards:** 82,000 exp and 700 eons

Special Tea is a quest given by Old Hermit in Anundo. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Anundo
   Special Tea
   Anundo
   Old Hermit
   Level 20
   82,000 exp and 700 eons
2. Go to Anundo and talk to the Old Hermit
4. Go to Despair port and follow the maze
5. Right, down, down, right, right, right, down, right, up
6. Talk to the Mysterious Hermit
7. He needs cinnamon from Shaman Lilo in Tropica
8. Shaman Lilo asks to kill 20 crabs in exchange for cinnamon
10. Go to Haunted Hallows
11. The stinky hallow root be found at the base of the trees on the same map as the Haunted House
12. Hover your mouse over the base of each tree to find the item laying on the ground
13. You can also use
   #range
   to find the coordinates with your minimap
14. Take the root back to the Mysterious Hermit at Despair
15. He makes the tea for you
16. Go back to Anundo and turn in the tea to the Old Hermit for 82,000 exp and 700 eons
