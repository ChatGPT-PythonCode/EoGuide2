**Location:** Anundo  
**Quest Giver:** Panda Kopan  
**Level Requirement:** 3  
**Rewards:** 300 exp and class

Destiny (rep) is a quest given by Panda Kopan in Anundo. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Travel to Anundo
2. You can follow the video below or keep scrolling for the visual tutorial
3. If starting in Aeven follow these steps:
   ·      Go north to enter Aeven North Entrance
4. ·      Before reaching the Aeven Mines, head east into Green Lake
5. ·      Travel north through the Swamp
6. ·      Take the lift
7. Tip:
   Dash is highly recommended for this section
8. You can purchase it from the Skills Wizard in Aeven when you reach level 3
9. After the lift, head north into the next map
10. (Beware: The Hyper Carnivor is extremely fast and deals heavy damage
11. Move west to the stairway, avoiding the Hyper Carnivor, and enter the stairs
12. Solve the staircase puzzle by following this path:
   South  North  Southeast
13. Here, go south
14. Here, go north
15. Here, go southeast
16. Continue north to enter Anundo
17. Use the map for reference points within Anundo
18. Enter the house on the west side of the map (coordinates 16,31)
19. Talk to Panda Kopan
20. He gives you 6 options for classes
21. The best class for you would depend on your current level and gear, but people usually opt for warrior, paladin, or archer
22. Choose the class that is right for you, and Panda Kopan will give you instructions on how to reach the shrine
23. Below are videos on how to get to each shrine
24. Once you reach your desired shrine, make sure to sit (F11) by the candles until you get a pop-up message
25. Now, you've successfully changed your class
26. You can always go back to Panda Kopan to change your class as many times as you need to
