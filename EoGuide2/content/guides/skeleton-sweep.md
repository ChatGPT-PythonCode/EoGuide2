Skeleton SweepLocation: Aeven EntombmentQuest Giver: Royal ScoutLevel Requirement:  8Reward: 4,000 exp and 400 eonsHead to the Aeven Entombment (South of the Castle) and find the Royal Scout.Speak with the Royal Scout who's outside of the Aeven Catacombs.He will ask you to defeat 20 Skeletons within the Catacombs.Enter the Aeven Catacombs and defeat 20 Skeletons.  All the Skeletons count towards the kill count.Once you defeated 20 Skeletons return to the Royal Scout to claim your reward of 4,000 exp and 400 eons.
