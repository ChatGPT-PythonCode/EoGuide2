2. WHAT YOU NEED:
5 timber (100 wood logs needed to craft), 10 sand stones, and 10 glass (40 sand needed to craft)
Take Aeven sealiner 101 to Josand
3. As soon as you get off the boat, Certy Sandi is inside the most northwest house
4. A sandstorm struck his house when gathering materials to fix the damage from the previous one
5. He says the missing blueprints are near the factory cave
6. You can find the missing Blueprints next to the cave that leads to the factory
7. Leave the house and go north 2 maps then west 1 map
8. Take the Blueprint to Certy Sandi
9. He then asks for some building materials listed on the blueprint
10. First thing he needs is 5 Timber
11. Give 5 timber to Certy
12. It’s 100 wood logs to craft 5 timber in the Aeven Toolshop
13. Certy then asks for 10 Sand Stones
14. Defeat the desert NPCs around the desert where you found the Blueprints to collect them
15. The last thing he needs is 10 Glass
16. Which requires 40 Sand to craft in the Aeven Forge
17. Sand can be collected in the Aeven Mines or Atlantis Big Ocean Map
18. After turning in all of the materials, Certy rewards you with 64,000 exp and 4,000 eons
