Josand
Scorpion ProblemQuest Giver: Stung GuardMap: Josand Desert & AevenRule: Defeat 16 scorpions, collect 4 tails, deliver antidoteReward: 110,000 Exp + 700 Eons1. Once you arrive to the Josand port, go 2 maps north to the bridge and find the Stung Guard on the left just before the bridge.2. Speak with the guard and he will tell you that everytime he walks to town he gets attacked by scorpions and asks you to clear them out.
Select "Will handle the scorpions",
"On my way"3. Go south to the map below and kill 16 Scorpions and collect 4 Scorpion tails.4. Go back to the Stung Guard who will tell you to hurry to the Witch in Aeven and get the antidote she has made for him.5.Go to Aeven and speak to the Witch she will agree to make the antidote but says you will owe her a favor
Select "Ok, Deal"She will then give you the antidote.6. Return to the Stung Guard in Josand and give him the antidote to complete the quest.
