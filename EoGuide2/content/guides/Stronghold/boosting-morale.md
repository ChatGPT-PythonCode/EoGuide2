**Location:** Aeven Battleground

**Level Requirement:** Unknown  
**Rewards:** 116,000 exp and 600 eon

Boosting Morale is a quest given by Big Commander in Aeven Battleground. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Aeven
   Stronghold
   Boosting Morale
   Aeven Battleground
   Big Commander
   116,000 exp and 600 eon
   Lvl requirement:
   5 big fairysodas, 1 omelette plate (3 eggs, 5 bottles of milk {which is 5 empty bottles & 15 empty buckets})
2. At the Aeven Battleground east of Stronghold, go to the tent at coordinates
   20, 38 and talk to the Big commander
3. 2.The commander asks you to talk to the tired soldiers by the fireplace
4. 3.The tired soldiers are directly behind the big commander tent, at coordinates 9,38
5. They ask you to kill 15 rats
6. 4.After killing the 15 rats, they will ask you for 5 big fairysodas which you can get at the Aeven pub right by the Aeven square
7. 5.After taking the 5 big fairysodas to the soldiers, go back to the big commander
8. He will then ask you for 1 omelette plate from the Aeven Market
9. 1 omelette plate requires 5 bottles of milk and 3 eggs
10. You can gather the eggs at 4 different spots at the Aeven Market
11. Before leaving the Aeven market, buy 10 empty buckets and 5 empty bottles
12. At the milk farm map (1 map west of Aeven town), go up to a cow and fill 10 buckets with milk
13. Then, go to the house at coordinates 21,15 to craft the milk bottles
14. 6.Go back to the Aeven Market and craft the omelette plate
15. After this quest is done, talk to the Big Commander again for the next quest - Iron Help
   Aeven
   Stronghold
   Iron Help

## Completion

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **116,000 EXP, 600 Eons**.
