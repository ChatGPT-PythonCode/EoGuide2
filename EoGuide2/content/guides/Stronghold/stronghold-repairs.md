**Location:** Aeven Stronghold  
**Quest Giver:** Soldier Mike  
**Level Requirement:** 1  
**Rewards:** 720 exp and 100 eons

Stronghold Repairs is a quest given by Soldier Mike in Aeven Stronghold. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Go to the Aeven Stronghold, located on the northeast of Aeven, and talk to Soldier Mike to start the quest
2. Soldier Mike explains he needs help gathering 5 Wood Logs to repair the catapult
3. Head south into the Stronghold Woods and collect 5 Wood Logs
4. Return to Soldier Mike with the logs
