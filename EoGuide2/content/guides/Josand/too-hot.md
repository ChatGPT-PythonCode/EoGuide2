**Location:** Josand Port  
**Quest Giver:** Hannibal Toohot  
**Level Requirement:** 26  
**Rewards:** 66,000 exp

Too Hot is a quest given by Hannibal Toohot in Josand Port. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Josand
   Too Hot
   Josand Port
   Hannibal Toohot
   Level 26
   66,000 exp
   3 Buckets of Pure Water
2. If you already have 3 Buckets of Pure Water, skip to Step 3
3. If not, go to the Aeven Market and talk to Cara Craft Merchant to buy 30–40 Empty Buckets
4. Travel to the Stronghold Woods east of Aeven and use the well by the road
5. ·      Each time you fill an Empty Bucket, you have a 10% chance to obtain a Bucket of Pure Water
6. ·      Repeat until you collect 3 Buckets of Pure Water
7. Once you have all 3, head to the Josand Port by taking the Aeven Sealiner 101
8. Go north in Josand Port and talk to Hannibal Toohot to start the quest
9. Hannibal Toohot explains he is struggling with the Josand heat and asks if you brought any water
10. Give him 1 Bucket of Pure Water, then offer 2 more
   Josand
   Turkey
   Scorpion Problem
   Storm Damage
   Desert Herb Hunt

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **Unknown**.
