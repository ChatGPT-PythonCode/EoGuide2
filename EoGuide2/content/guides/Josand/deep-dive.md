**Location:** Josand Town  
**Quest Giver:** Shabby "The Big Guy"  
**Level Requirement:** 26  
**Rewards:** 92,000 exp

Deep Dive is a quest given by Shabby "The Big Guy" in Josand Town. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

2. 1.Go to Shabby in Josand
3. He tells you he’s always been intrigued about the mysteries of Atlantis
4. The pathway had collapsed, and the only way to go there is with a submarine
5. Now, the submarine needs essential parts to fix it
6. He sends you to Aeven to talk to Shady, who sold you the submarine
7. Go to Shady Doorkeeper in Aeven and ask him for the missing parts
8. He gives you a submarine exhaust and says the rest of the parts were sold to someone else for some eons
9. Travel back to Josand to talk to Shabby
10. He needs more help to resolve the submarine issues and invites you to go to Atlantis with him
11. The issue will be fixed after going to the inventor
12. Go to the inventor in the desert (bunker 5) through the cave north of Aeven
13. He gives you a card that gives you access to the storage room inside the factory basement
14. Travel back to Josand and go to the factory
15. Inside, go to the northwest corner and enter into the elevator
16. In the basement, go to the west (left) part of the map and enter the door
17. You’ll find the submarine driver on the floor in the next map
18. Give the submarine driver to Shabby
19. He tells you to consult with the Captain to see if they are ready to depart with the submarine
20. Captain Scuba says the engine is starting but it’s probably not safe
21. You get transported inside the sub
22. Something is wrong - check the engine at the bottom of the map simply by standing in front of it
23. Talk to Captain scuba and tell him a couple engine parts are missing
24. Then he tells you he needs you to look for some of the submarine parts outside
25. Find the 3 missing parts outside
26. They are all being guarded by Devil Fish
27. The rotor shaft is in the northwest corner
28. The left rotor is south
29. The right rotor is northeast
30. Return to the submarine with the 3 parts
31. Captain Scuba asks you to install them - walk to the south of the map like you did earlier
32. Then, he sets sail for Atlantis
33. Shabby thanks you for your time and hints at the next quest available at King Neptune
