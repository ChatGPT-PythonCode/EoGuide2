Desert Herb Hunt is a quest. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. First we go to Josand, from Aeven Port take SeaLiner 101 (first boat)
2. Once off the boat in Josand walk north untill you reach the next map, once on the next map take a left into Josand City and located the Herb Merchant in the city square
3. Speak with the Herb Merchant and she will ask you foe 10 Dryweed, Agree and finish the dialog
4. Leave the map going back to the east and return to the previous map and take one of the two staircases to the north
5. You will come out on the top left of the map then go north to the next map
6. Collect 10 dryweed!
   Here are all the places you can find dryweed

   Dryweed Restock (Daily)
