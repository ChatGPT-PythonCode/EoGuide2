Suspicious Pots is a quest given by Shubba Duna in Josand Big Mansion. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Go to the Josland Big Mansion, located on the north side of Josand Town
2. Talk to Shubba Duna to start the quest
3. Shubba Duna explains that strange noises and missing items have been reported in people’s homes, and he asks you to investigate
4. Leave the mansion and use the
   #range
   command outside to locate the coordinates of a Suspicious Pot
5. Go to the marked coordinates with your mini-map open
6. Interact with the Suspicious Pot
7. A Ninja Spy will jump out and attack you — defeat the Ninja Spy
8. Return to Shubba Duna and report the discovery of the Spy Ninjas
9. Shubba Duna asks you to continue investigating
10. Go back outside, use
   #range
   again, and locate another Suspicious Pot
11. Interact with the pot and defeat the Spy Ninja once again
