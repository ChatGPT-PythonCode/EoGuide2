Scorpion Problem is a quest given by Stung Guard. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Josand
   Scorpion Problem
   Map: Josand Desert & Aeven
   Rule: Defeat 16 scorpions, collect 4 tails, deliver antidote
2. Once you arrive to the Josand port, go 2 maps north to the bridge and find the Stung Guard on the left just before the bridge
3. Speak with the guard and he will tell you that everytime he walks to town he gets attacked by scorpions and asks you to clear them out
4. Select - "Will handle the scorpions",
   "On my way"
5. Go south to the map below and kill 16 Scorpions and collect 4 Scorpion tails
6. Go back to the Stung Guard who will tell you to hurry to the Witch in Aeven and get the antidote she has made for him
7. 5.Go to Aeven and speak to the Witch she will agree to make the antidote but says you will owe her a favor
   Select - "Ok, Deal"
   She will then give you the antidote
8. Return to the Stung Guard in Josand and give him the antidote to complete the quest

## Completion

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **110,000 EXP, 700 Eons**.
