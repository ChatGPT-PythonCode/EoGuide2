Reactor Rescue is a quest. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Josand
   Reactor Rescue
   NOTE: Must complete quest
   Factory SOS
   first to unlock Reactor Rescue
2. 1.Head to the factory and speak to Alpha Terminal
3. He tells you to go to the first floor, which is accessed by the elevator directly straight (west) upon entering into the factory map
4. Head inside the factory and take the elevator up to the first floor
5. The elevator entrance is at coordinate 6,34
6. At the first floor, the Alpha Terminal tells you that each elevator that gives access to the other floors in the factory require keycard access
7. Go through each maze on each floor, and find the operator that drops the keycard
8. Finish the 1st, 2nd, and 3rd floor maze
9. Make sure to stay on each floor until you have each keycard
10. On the 4th and final floor of the factory, Alpha Terminal needs you go to the control panel at the end of that floor
11. Simply walk up to it
12. Talk to Alpha Terminal again
13. He prompts you to go to the reactor room at the basement of the factory to meet him
14. Make sure you talk to him again to activate a different quest, Super Intruder
15. Josand
   Super Intruder
   Factory SOS
