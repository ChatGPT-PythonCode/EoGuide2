**Location:** Shadow Church  
**Quest Giver:** Devilish Shade  
**Level Requirement:** Unknown  
**Rewards:** Chance at Devil Costume and Baby Blue

Devil Chest (rep) is a quest given by Devilish Shade in Shadow Church. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Travel to the Shadow Border, the map containing both the Portal to Hell and the Shadow Church
2. Enter the Shadow Church, located just south of the Portal to Hell
3. Walk west and talk to the Devilish Shade
4. He instructs you to defeat 66 Imps and collect 3 Demon Souls
5. Exit the church, head north, and enter the Portal to Hell
6. Defeat 66 Imps while also farming for 3 Demon Souls
7. ·
   Drop rates:
   Demon Souls have a 6% chance from Walking Imps and 10% chance from Flying Imps
8. Once you’ve met the requirements, leave Hell and re-enter the Shadow Church
9. Speak to the Devilish Shade again, and he will teleport you to the Devil Chest
10. Each attempt at the Devil Chest works as follows
11. ·
   Every 10 attempts increase your bonus chance by 0.01%
12. ·
   At 666 attempts, the drop becomes guaranteed
13. In addition to the Devil Costume, you also have a chance to obtain
   Baby Blue
   ,
   and was adjusted again in
   Port of Shadows
   Turkey
   Shade Request
   Last Redemption
   Imp Redemption

## Completion

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **Unknown**.
