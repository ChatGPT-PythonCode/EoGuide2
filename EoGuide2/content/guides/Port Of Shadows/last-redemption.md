**Location:** Shadow Border  
**Quest Giver:** Fallen Shade  
**Level Requirement:** 30  
**Rewards:** 30,000 exp

Last Redemption is a quest given by Fallen Shade in Shadow Border. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Port of Shadows
   Last Redemption
   Shadow Border
   Fallen Shade
   Level 30
   30,000 exp
   Note:
   The quest
   Imp Redemption
   (minimum level 30) is a prerequisite to start this quest
2. Go to Aeven Port and sail to the Port Of Shadows, and head to the main town
3. Once there locate Fallen Shade (from imp redemption quest)
4. He can be found behind the gate to hell
5. Speak with Fallen Shade and he will tell you there is another threat and requests you to go into hell and defeat 3 Hell Guardians
6. SIDE NOTE
   : These guys are tuff, I'm level 78 at the time of writing this and it took me around 20mins to solo 3 of these so it's DEFINITELY best to do this one in a group or with a tank
7. Go into the gates of hell and kill 3 Hell Guardians
8. Once you have killed all 3 return to Fallen Shade to complete quest
   Port of Shadows
   Shade Request
   Imp Redemption

## Completion

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **Unknown**.
