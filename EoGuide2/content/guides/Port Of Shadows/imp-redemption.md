**Location:** Shadow Border  
**Quest Giver:** Fallen Shade  
**Level Requirement:** 30  
**Rewards:** 90,000 exp

Imp Redemption is a quest given by Fallen Shade in Shadow Border. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. 1.Go to the Shadow Border and talk to Fallen Shade right outside the door to hell
2. He asks you to kill 60 Imps
3. Go through the portal and defeat 60 Flying or Walking Imps!
4. He'll then give you another quest
   Last Redemption
   Port of Shadows
   Devil Chest (rep)
   Shade Request
   Last Redemption

## Completion

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **Unknown**.
