Theatre PincodeLocation: Aeven TheatreQuest Giver: Aeven MagicianLevel Requirement:  Level 1Reward: 1,200 exp and 100 eonsNote: The quest Showtime is a prerequisite to start this quest.Go to the Aeven Theatre and talk to the Aeven Magician to start the quest. (Aeven Theatre is where the Bank used to be)The Aeven Magician wants you to look around the theatre and investigate.Go north of the Aeven Magician and speak to the Chicken CC.Select the Honest? option.Select the You Seem Nervous Option.A in-game window will pop up informing you to spy from a distance.Stand in this location and wait for the Chicken CC to jump the counter and move north.Follow the Chicken CC and watch him enter a code and enter the northern door.Walk up to the door and another in-game window will pop up.Enter the northeast door. Grid 18,8 Speak to Chicken CC again.After speaking with Chicken CC return to the Aeven Magician to claim your reward of 1,200 exp and 100 eons.
