**Location:** Hallodale

**Level Requirement:** 30  
**Rewards:** 43,000 exp and 240 eons

Lost Cargo is a quest given by Lost Merchant (Wendy Waters is a typo) in Hallodale. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Hallodale
   Lost Cargo
   Hallodale
   Lost Merchant (Wendy Waters is a typo)
   Level 30
   43,000 exp and 240 eons
2. Take the train to Hallodale, once you get off the train go east one map and find the Lost Merchant
3. He will tell you that the cargo for his shop has been lost and request that you help find it!
4. Accept the quest and then head back right to the Train Station map
5. Once on the map use the
   #range
   command to locate the exact tile the cargo can be found
6. (in this case it was located at (x8 - y53)
7. Once you locate the cargo walk up to it and click on it to collect, once you have it in your inventory go back to town and speak with the Lost Merchant again to complete quest
   Hallodale
   Lost Kitty
   Trouble in Port
   Buzz Off

## Completion

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **43,000 EXP, 240 Eons**.
