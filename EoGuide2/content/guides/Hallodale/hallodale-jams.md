**Location:** Hallodale  
**Quest Giver:** Plinky  
**Level Requirement:** 30  
**Rewards:** 22,000 exp and 5 Music Books

Hallodale Jams is a quest given by Plinky in Hallodale. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Go to the Aeven railroads and purchase a train ticket to Hallodale
2. Give the ticket to the conductor and go to Hallodale station
3. Once you're at Hallodale station, head east one map to enter Hallodale
4. Plinky can be found wandering around the statue near 24,54
5. Talk to Plinky he's going to ask for eons multiple times
6. Give Plinky 60 eons
7. Give Plinky 60 eons...again
8. Yep, a 4th time
9. Give him 1 last tip of 60 eons
