**Location:** Hallodale Port  
**Quest Giver:** Benny Barrelwood  
**Level Requirement:** 30  
**Rewards:** 80,000 exp and 200 eons

Trouble in Port is a quest given by Benny Barrelwood in Hallodale Port. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

**What you'll need:** A Fishing Rod

1. Take the train to Hallodale, once you get off the train go east one map and then south one map
2. Once there continue south and find Benny Barrelwood
3. Talk to Benny Berrelwood and he'll explain his barrels have gone missing
4. He will tell you to find Wendy Waters
5. Head south and talk to Wendy Waters
6. Then, talk to Sab
7. There's a bit of back-and-forth dialogue
8. Sab will tell you to fish 3 barrels from the water with a fishing rod
   Hallodale
   Buzz Off
   Shady Swap

## Completion

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **80,000 EXP, 200 Eons**.
