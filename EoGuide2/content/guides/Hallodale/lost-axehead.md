**Location:** Hallodale North  
**Quest Giver:** Woodcutter  
**Level Requirement:** 30  
**Rewards:** 96,000 exp and 3 Wood Logs

Lost Axehead is a quest given by Woodcutter in Hallodale North. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Take the train to Hallodale, once you get off the train go east one map and then north one map
2. Once there immediately go west and find the Woodcutter
3. He will explain he lost his axe and needs you to help him find it
4. The Lost Axehead will be on one the following maps:
   ·       One map north of where the Woodcutter is
5. (Ant Map)
   ·       West of Hollodale Town at the Hallodale Railroads
6. ·       Or east of Hallodale Town on the Hallodale Beverdam map
7. Walk around those maps, and you can use
   #range
   to help you find it
8. We found one at 13, 13 on the Hallodale North map next to the ant nest
9. We also found one on 36, 5 on the Hollodale Railroad map
10. If you're level 40 make sure you talk to him again to start the next quest in the questline,
   Ant Trouble
   Hallodale
   Lumber Numbers
   Ant Trouble
   Lost Cargo

## Completion

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **Unknown**.
