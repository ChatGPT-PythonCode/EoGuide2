**Location:** Hallodale North  
**Quest Giver:** Woodcutter  
**Level Requirement:** 40  
**Rewards:** 102,000 exp and 600 eons

Ant Trouble is a quest given by Woodcutter in Hallodale North. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Take the train to Hallodale, once you get off the train go east one map and then north one map
2. Once there immediately go west and find the Woodcutter
3. He will ask you to defeat 60 Ants
4. Accept and then go north one map to the Ant Nest once there defeat 60 Ant Workers/ Ant Soldiers
   Hallodale
   Lumber Numbers
   Lost Axehead
   Buzz Off

## Completion

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **102,000 EXP, 600 Eons**.
