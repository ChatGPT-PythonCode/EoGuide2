**Location:** Hallodale North  
**Quest Giver:** Woody Sinwood  
**Level Requirement:** 30  
**Rewards:** 82,000 exp and 400 eons

Buzz Off is a quest given by Woody Sinwood in Hallodale North. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Take the train to Hallodale, once you get off the train go east one map and then north one map
2. Once there immediately go west and find Woody Sinwood
3. Talk to Woody Sinwood he will ask you to kill 60 Wasp/ Stingers
4. Go north on the current and next map and kill 60 Wasp/ Stingers

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **82,000 EXP, 400 Eons**.
