Lost Kitty is a quest given by Penny Paws in Hallodale. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Hallodale
   Lost Kitty
   Hallodale
   Penny Paws
   Level 30
   71,000 exp and 1 Buddy Coin
2. Go to Hallodale, once off the train go east one map and continue east until you see Penny Paws sitting beside the fence
3. Penny says that her little kitty has gone missing, and she needs help finding him
4. Go south 1 map to the Hallodale Port
5. While walking around the map use the command
   #range
   to find the Lost Kitty
6. Once you find the Lost Kitty click on them to pick them up and return to Penny Paws
