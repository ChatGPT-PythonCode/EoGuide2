**Location:** Hallodale North  
**Quest Giver:** Woodcutter  
**Level Requirement:** 40  
**Rewards:** 80,000 exp and 400 eons

Lumber Numbers is a quest given by Woodcutter in Hallodale North. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. After finishing Ant Trouble, talk to the Woodcutter again to start the quest
2. Or if you're returning
   take the train to Hallodale, once you get off the train go east one map and then north one map
3. Once there immediately go west and find the Woodcutter
4. Talk to the Woodcutter and he will ask you multiple questions
5. The answers for his questions are:
   Ant Nest area - 10 log piles
   Hallodale town - 10 log piles
   Hallodale port - 9 log piles
   Beverdam - 4 log piles
   Once you answer everything correctly, he asks you to visit the Wood Forman at the Hallodale Port
6. Go to the Hallodale Port by going south 2 maps then you can find him on the east side of the port at coordinates 52,18
7. Talk to the Wood Forman
   Hallodale
   Ant Trouble
   Lost Axehead
   Lost Cargo

## Completion

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **80,000 EXP, 400 Eons**.
