Fuel the Fire is a quest that tasks you with assisting Shady in Snowlandia. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Snowlandia
Fuel the Fire
WHAT YOU NEED:
3 timber (needs 60 wood logs to craft them all)
2. Take the Aeven Airliner 121 to Snowlandia
3. From the Snowlandia port, take the elevator down to go to Snowlandia Town
4. Enter into the house right below the clothing shop in the southeast of the town
5. Talk to Cody and he will tell you to inspect the fireplaces
6. Walk up to the fireplaces on the west wall
7. Talk to Cody again
8. He will tell you to persuade Shady Doorkeeper in Aeven to allow him inside the tool shop to place a large wood order
9. Go to Aeven and talk to Shady Doorman to vouch for Cody
10. Go back to Cody, He asks you to get 3 timber
11. In the Aeven tool shop (behind bank) use 60 wood logs to craft 15 wood, and then craft 3 timber
12. Bring the 3 timber to Cody to receive a reward of 40,000 xp and 2,200 eon
13. Snowlandia
One-Time Quest
Ice Flowers
Snowballs
Oh, what fun

## Completion

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **Unknown**.
