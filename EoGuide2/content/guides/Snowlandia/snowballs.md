Snowballs is a quest given by Jake Frost in Snowlandia Town. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. 1.From the Aeven spawn area, go up the elevator
2. This will take you to the Aeven airdock
3. Purchase an air ticket and go to Snowlandia
4. Once you're at Snowlandia, go down the elevator to arrive at Snowlandia Town
5. At Snowlandia Town, talk to Jake Frost
6. 2.He asks you to gather 16 snowballs
7. They are scattered around Snowlandia town
8. Turn on your minimap by pressing Tab on your keyboard to see the coordinates on the map
