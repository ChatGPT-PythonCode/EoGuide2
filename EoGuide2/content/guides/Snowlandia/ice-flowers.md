**Location:** Snowlandia Town  
**Quest Giver:** Elsi Snowflake  
**Level Requirement:** 12  
**Rewards:** 26,000 exp and 450 eons

Ice Flowers is a quest given by Elsi Snowflake in Snowlandia Town. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. 1.From the Aeven spawn area, go up the elevator
2. This will take you to the Aeven airdock
3. Purchase an air ticket, and go to Snowlandia
4. Once you're at Snowlandia, go down the elevator
5. Once you're in Snowlandia town, directly south of the elevator is Elsi Snowflake
6. 2.She asks for 6 ice flowers
7. You can gather them inside the ice cave, directly east (right) of Elsi
8. 3.Gather 6 ice flowers around the ice caves
9. You will need 2 warmth items (hat, gloves, and/or Warm Robes) to avoid receiving damage from the cold every few seconds
10. If you've roamed around and gathered a few, you can step outside to heal before going back inside the ice caves
11. Completing this quest also unlocks the next quest, Oh what fun
   Snowlandia
   Snowballs
   Oh, what fun
12. Fuel the Fire

## Completion

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **26,000 EXP, 450 Eons**.
