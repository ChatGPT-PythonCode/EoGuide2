## Walkthrough

1. Nostalgia
   Identity
2. Take Sealiner 106 to Nostalgia
3. Once you get off the boat, follow the path to the west and then go to the next map and enter into the house to the left of the water well
4. Talk to Not Mommy
5. She will prompt you to ask around town for her
6. *Side note, the chest inside this room is your bank
7. Now walk out and go into the house on the right side of the water well
8. Go inside and talk to Trudy
9. Click on:
   Ask about Goat Horn Collector
10. Go to the Goat Horn Collector in the Aeven market and
   ask about Nostalgia
11. Nostalgia
   Hatching Mogui (daily)
   Owl Awakening (daily)
   From Ash to Den
