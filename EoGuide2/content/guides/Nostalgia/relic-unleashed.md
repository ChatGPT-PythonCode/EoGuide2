**Location:** Nostalgia Edge  
**Quest Giver:** Dragon Collector  
**Level Requirement:** 45  
**Rewards:** 150,000 exp and a Tome of Master Knowledge

Relic Unleashed is a quest given by Dragon Collector in Nostalgia Edge. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Nostalgia
   Relic Unleashed
   Nostalgia Edge
   Dragon Collector
   Level 45
   150,000 exp and a Tome of Master Knowledge
   Note:
   Knowledge isn't 100% implemented in the game yet
2. Its best to not use these Tomes until that part of the game is developed more
3. Go to Nostalgia and head to the southwest part of the Island
4. Right outside the Dragon Den Cave and speak with the Dragon Collector
5. She will tell you that the Relic you recovered for her is making a hum and whispers to her and she will ask you to go to the Port of Shadows to drop the relic into the volcano to destroy it
6. Go to the Port of Shadows, once off the boat head north to the cave entrance, instead of going into the cave, go around it on the right side until you find another door on the west wall
7. Go into it, once you come out on the next map go north to the next door, then on the last map go southwest to the final door on the north wall
8. Once at the top walk to the north side of the volcano and step onto one of the pillows to throw the relic in and destroy it
9. Return to Nostalgia Island and speak with the Dragon Collector again to complete the quest
   Nostalgia
   Dragon Collector

## Completion

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **Unknown**.
