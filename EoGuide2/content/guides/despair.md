**Location:** Woods Of Despair  
**Quest Giver:** Concerned  
**Level Requirement:** 5  
**Rewards:** 6,700 EXP, 600 Eons

Despair is a quest that tasks you with assisting Concerned in Woods Of Despair. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Woods of Despair
Despair

Port of Despair

Concerned Mindy

Level 5
Reward:
6,700 exp and 600 eons
What You'll Need:
2 Boat Tickets
Go to Port of Despair, to get there go to the Aeven Port and find the Retired Captain on the southernmost part of the port
2. (You'll need two boat tickets)
Once there go slightly north and talk to Concerned Mindy to start the quest
3. Once you have finished talking with Mindy go north into the Woods of Despair
4. This is a maze you need to go:
North, South, South, East, East, East, South, East, North or
Up, Down, Down, Right, Right, Right, Down, Right, Up
5. When you come out of the woods walk north and enter the house at the top of the map
6. Speak with the Mysterious Hermit
7. Once you're done talking with the Hermit leave the building and go back into the woods and go south, you will come out at the Port of Despair
8. Speak with Concerned Mindy again and claim your reward of 6,700 exp and 600 eons
