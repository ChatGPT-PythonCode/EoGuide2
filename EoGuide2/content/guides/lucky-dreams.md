Lucky DreamsLocation: DreamworldQuest Giver: BatmasoLevel Requirement:  Level 32Reward: 77,777 exp and Lucky CharmMake your way to the Aeven Inn, directly south of Aeven.Inside, purchase a dream key from the Inn Hostess.Go through these doors.And then... these doors.5. Sit on the bed for a few seconds.Follow the path to the Batmaso NPC. He asks you to rescue 10 Twinkles.From here, Batmaso will warp you to the maze. The video below (begin at timestamp 2:34) will show you the way.Alternatively, you can follow the images below for the 2 maze maps.Map 1Map 2Once the maze is over, make sure to sit on the beds.
*CAREFUL make sure you have potions on you, and a shield equipped. The bogomen hit hard (pun intended) and if you die, you will lose your maze progress.
The twinkles are in 3 spots within the cages. They respawn pretty quickly (between 1-2 minutes approximately.)Once you have 10, return to Batmaso and he will give you a lucky charm as well as 77,777 exp. After this one-time quest is completed, it becomes a repeatable quest for 33,333 exp per round.
