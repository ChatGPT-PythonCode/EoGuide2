Piecing The Puzzle is a quest that tasks you with assisting Darla in Tropica Island. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Tropica
Piecing The Puzzle

Tropica Pirate Cove
Quest Giver:
Darla Whisper

Reward:
80,000 exp
Go to Pirate Cove on Tropica Island and talk to Darla Whisper to start the quest
2. She will ask you to help her by talking to the Pirate With Shovel
3. You can find him standing right next to Darla Whisper
4. Speak with him and he'll ask you to speak with Penny Stock
5. You can find her to the southeast of the Pirate With Shovel
6. Speak with Penny Stock who will then ask you to speak with Golden Joe
7. Golden Joe is standing right next to Penny Stock
8. Speak with Golden Joe and he'll ask you to talk to Dex Deckhand
9. Dex Deckhand can be found on the pirate ship just to the west of Golden Joe
10. Speak with Dex Deckhand and he'll ask you to talk to Daniel Daggers
11. Head north of the pirate ship and enter the building north of Pirate With Shovel
12. Daniel Daggers is standing in the corner of the pub
13. Speak with Daniel Daggers and he'll then ask you to speak with Barry Booz
14. Barry Booz is standing behind the counter
15. He'll then ask you to go back outside and speak with Pirate With Shovel again
16. Pirate With Shovel will then ask you to speak with Daniel Daggers again
17. Go back to the bar and find Daniel Daggers
18. He'll then ask you to talk to Penny Stock
19. Head southeast of the pub and talk to Penny Stock
20. She'll ask you to talk to Golden Joe
21. Golden Joe will then ask you to talk to Barry Booz
22. Return to the pub and speak with Barry Booz
23. Barry will ask you to go back to Dex Deckhand on the pirate ship
24. Head south of the pub and talk to Dex Deckhand
25. Dex will then ask you to report back to Darla Whisper
26. Head northwest and talk to Darka Whisper
27. After speaking with her you can claim your reward of 80,000 exp
