Needle in the Hay is a quest. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Take Sealiner 106 to Centaur and go north until you reach the town
2. On the west side of the town, enter the last house
3. Talk to Mindyrella
4. She will ask you to find a needle in a haystack
5. The needle can be found in any haystack around Centaur town, the map to the left of centaur town, the map south of the town, and the map you arrived from the port
6. Hover your mouse on top of each haystack until you find the item
7. Use the
   #range
   function with your minimap on - this will save you time
8. Once you've found it, go back and give the needle to Mindyrella
   Hoofing It (rep)
   Stallion
