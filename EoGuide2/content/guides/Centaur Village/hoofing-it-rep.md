**Level Requirement:** 24  
**Rewards:** 4,500 exp and 80 eons

Hoofing It (rep) is a quest given by Centaur Cita in Centaur Outhouse. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Head to Centaur Cita inside the Centaur Outhouse
2. From Aeven, go south to the Aeven Port and talk to the Boat Captain
3. Then select Centaur Port
4. Once you arrive at Centaur Port, head north two maps, then west one map, and enter the house to the north
5. Inside, find Centaur Cita
6. Speak to Centaur Cita
7. She asks you to gather 10 Horseshoes
8. Horseshoes can be obtained by defeating several different types of Centaurs
