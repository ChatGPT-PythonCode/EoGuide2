**Location:** Centaur Wine Farm  
**Quest Giver:** Centaur Queen  
**Level Requirement:** 15  
**Rewards:** 42,000 exp and 300 eons

Strong Brew is a quest given by Centaur Queen in Centaur Wine Farm. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Keep going north until you reach Centaur Village and enter the Centaur Wine Farm
2. Once inside
   speak to the Centaur Queen
3. She will ask you to gather 30 Brews from the cellar
4. Go north and into the basement
5. Retrieve 30 Gatherable Brew from the cellar
6. There are 6 spots scattered throughout the map
7. Once you collected 30 return to the Centaur Queen
8. She will prompt you to go see Centaur Cita
9. Leave the house and go outside
10. Go west to the next map, and Centaur Cita is in the very first house
11. She will give you a brew to put in the cellar wine rack
12. Go back to the wine cellar map where you gathered the 30 brews and walk all the way north
13. By walking up against the wall, it will place the wine on the cellar
14. Once you get the pop-up, return to the Centaur Queen
15. Return to the Centaur Queen, she will prompt you to collect 10 Horseshoes for her guards
16. Defeating the regular Centaur outside of the Centaur Queens house would be the safe and one of the fastest ways to acquire these
   Needle in the Hay
   Stallion

## Completion

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **42,000 EXP, 300 Eons**.
