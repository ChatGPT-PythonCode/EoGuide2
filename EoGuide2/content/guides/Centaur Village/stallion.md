**Location:** Centaur Wine Farm  
**Quest Giver:** Centaur Queen  
**Level Requirement:** 15  
**Rewards:** Stallion Key

Stallion is a quest given by Centaur Queen in Centaur Wine Farm. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. 3 Iron
2. Speak to the Centaur Queen
3. She will ask you to bring her 3 Iron in exchange for a Stallion Key
4. This gives you access to the Stallion boss room
5. To get to the Stallion Boss room go north 1 map and then use the key on the northwest door

## Completion

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **Unknown**.
