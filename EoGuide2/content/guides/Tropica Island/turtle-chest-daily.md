**Location:** Tropica Beach  
**Quest Giver:** Turtle Ferryman  
**Level Requirement:** Unknown  
**Rewards:** Chance at Turtle Costume

Turtle Chest (daily) is a quest given by Turtle Ferryman in Tropica Beach. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Travel to the Aeven Port
2. Talk to Boatman and take the Aeven SeaLiner 101 to Tropica Port
3. On Tropica Island, head east two maps until you find the Turtle Ferryman sitting on the beach
4. You can follow the video below or continue scrolling for the image instructions
5. Pay him 300 eons and he will ferry you to Turtle Island
6. Open the Turtle Chest for a chance at the Turtle Costume
7. Each attempt at the Turtle Chest works as follows
8. ·
   Every 2 attempts increase your bonus chance by 0.01%
9. ·
   At 210 attempts, the drop becomes guaranteed
   and was adjusted again in
   Tropica
   Turkey
   Pirate Feud
   Piecing The Puzzle
   Supply Scourge (daily)

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **Unknown**.
