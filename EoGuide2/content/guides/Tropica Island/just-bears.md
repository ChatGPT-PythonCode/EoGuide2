**Location:** Tropica Bay  
**Quest Giver:** Sitting Bear  
**Level Requirement:** Unknown  
**Rewards:** 9,400 exp and the chance at Bad Bear (Bear Costume)

Just Bears is a quest given by Sitting Bear in Tropica Bay. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Tropica
   Just Bears
   Tropica Bay
   Sitting Bear
   9,400 exp and the chance at Bad Bear (Bear Costume)
2. Go to Tropica Island, once off the boat go North to the next map
3. Once on the next map continue north again, and on the right of that map youll see a group of bears sitting around
4. Talk to the Sitting Bear
5. Speak with the Bears then choose: "You are clearly guys in bear suits",  "Kinda Weird.." and then "Mind if I look around"
6. After speakng with the bears go to the sand castle just to the east of them and walk onto it
7. Once inside the hidden map speak with Sitting Bear to finish your quest
   Call of the Fur
   for a chance at getting the Bear Costume
   Tropica
   Call of the Fur (daily)
   Holy Entrance
   Coco Brothers

## Completion

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **Unknown**.
