**Location:** Tropica Pirate Cove

**Level Requirement:** Unknown  
**Rewards:** 200,000 exp and 2,500 eon

Pirate Feud is a quest given by Daniel Daggers in Tropica Pirate Cove. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Go to Pirate Cove on Tropica Island
2. Enter the house at 37, 23 and talk to Daniel Daggers  to start the quest
3. He asks you to defeat 50 invading pirates
4. These are the pirates that spawn
   only
   during the Pirate Attack world event at the Aeven Port
5. During the Pirate Attack, kill any of the pirates that spawn
6. Tips:
   to speed up this process, join a party with as many people as possible
7. This quest can take at least 2 to 3 Pirate Attack events to complete
8. If you are a lower level, make sure you bring potions with you, as these pirates can hit pretty hard
   Tropica
   Piecing The Puzzle
   Supply Scourge (daily)
   Pirates United

## Completion

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **200,000 EXP, 2,500 Eons**.
