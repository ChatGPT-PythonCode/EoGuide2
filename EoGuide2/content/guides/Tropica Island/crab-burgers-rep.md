**Location:** Tropica Village  
**Quest Giver:** Coco Ray  
**Level Requirement:** 20  
**Rewards:** 1,780 exp and 50 eons

Crab Burgers (rep) is a quest given by Coco Ray in Tropica Village. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Travel to the Aeven Port
2. Talk to Boatman and take the Aeven SeaLiner 101 to Tropica Port
3. Upon arrival at Tropica Island, head east one map, then north to reach Tropica Village and find Coco Ray
4. Speak to Coco Ray
5. He asks you to bring him 8 Crab Meat
6. Crabs can be found in:
   ·      Tropica Port
   ·      Tropica Bay
   ·      Tropica Beach
   Defeat them until you collect 8 Crab Meat
