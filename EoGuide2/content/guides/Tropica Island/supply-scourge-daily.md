5. If farming the drop, travel to the Aeven Port
6. Talk to Boatman and take the Aeven SeaLiner 101 to Tropica Port
7. Upon arrival at Tropica Island, head east one map, then north two maps to reach The Octemple
8. Continue north until you reach the Octo’s Lair
9. Defeat Octo until you get a Pirate Supply
10. Once you have the Pirate Supply, leave The Octemple and go north
11. On the beach, head east and find Penny Stock standing by the Pirate Ship
12. Speak with Penny Stock to complete the quest
