Teensy Bbq (rep) is a quest given by Coco Jane in Tropica Village. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

6. Speak to Coco Jane
7. She asks you to bring her 8 Teensy Meat
8. Teensy Meat can be collected from:

9. Continue further north to reach Octo’s Lair
