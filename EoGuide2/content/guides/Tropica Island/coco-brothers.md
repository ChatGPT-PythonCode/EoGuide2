## Walkthrough

   Coco Brothers

2. Head to the Aeven Port and take the boat to Tropica
3. Speak with Coconut J in the Tropica town center
4. He will ask you to return to Aeven and speak with his brother Coconut G who is at Aeven Beach
5. Speak with Coconut G and he will tell you to let his brother know that his Hula buiness is doing to good and he cannot leave
6. Return to Tropica and speak with Coconut J to complete the quest!
