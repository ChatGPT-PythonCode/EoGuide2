Holy Entrance is a quest given by Coco Jaime in Tropica. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. 1.Go to Tropica
2. Once you are off the boat, talk to Coco Jaime
3. He asks you to visit Coco Jen
4. Go 1 map to the east (right)
5. Then go 1 map to the north (up) to enter the Tropica Village
6. Coco Jenâ€™s house is at the northwest corner of the map, behind the town board
7. Talk to Coco Jen
8. She mentions a strange trail by the sea and wants you to investigate it
9. From the Tropica Village, go south (down)
10. At the next map, keep going south and simply walk up to the water
11. 5.Return to Coco Jen
12. She asks you to investigate the entrance of the Temple
13. From the Tropica Village, go north (up)
14. Simply walk up to the Temple entrance and then return to Coco Jen
15. Talk to Coco Jen
16. She mentions a horrible monster is inside the Holy Temple and asks if you are brave enough to see what happened
17. The temple is off limits and she will need to do a spiritual cleansing
18. For this, you will need 16 star flowers
19. Gather 16 star flowers
20. They are gatherable in the surrounding maps of Tropica
21. Then, go back to Coco Jen
22. To finish the spiritual cleansing, you must have the following equipped on your paper doll:
   1 tribal necklace
   2 tribal rings
   2 tribal bracelets
   2 tribal bracers
   You can purchase these at the southeast corner of Tropica Village
23. Proceed with the spiritual cleansing
24. Make sure to talk to her again before leaving to activate another quest, Chop Chop
