**Location:** Tropica Port  
**Quest Giver:** Coco Luna  
**Level Requirement:** 21  
**Rewards:** 2,200 exp

To The Rescue (rep) is a quest given by Coco Luna in Tropica Port. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Travel to the Aeven Port
2. Talk to the Boatman and take the Aeven SeaLiner 101 to Tropica Port
3. Upon arrival at Tropica Island, find Coco Luna just off the dock
4. Speak with her and she will ask you to collect 15 baby turtles
5. Baby Turtles can be found in:
   ·      Tropica Port
   ·      Tropica Bay
   ·      Tropica Beach
   ·      Turtle Island
   You simply click on them to collect them
6. After you've collected all 15 return to Coco Luna and turn them in to complete the quest!

   Teensy Bbq (rep)
   Crab Burgers (rep)
   Holy Entrance

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **Unknown**.
