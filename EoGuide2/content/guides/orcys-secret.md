**Prerequisites**\

A *Shrink Key* is required to enter the tiny door in *Shadow Desert (1)* where the quest is started. It can be crafted at *Witch Works* inside the *Creepy House in Aeven*. \
\
**The ingredients to craft a Shrink Key are:**\

**Empty Minor Potion Empty Minor Potion x**1 - Can be bought at the Bottle Shop in the Aeven Juice Shop for 10 Eon.
**Green Berry Green Berry x4** - Can be harvested from nodes at Aeven West and the Aeven Stronghold or traded for 2 yellow berries each at Barry's Berry Swap in the Aeven marketplace.
**Carrot Carrot x15** - Can be harvested from nodes at Aeven Outlet.
**Snake Rattle Snake Rattle x2** - Can be dropped from Snakes at Aeven Falls or Green Lake.\
\

**Walkthrough**\

Begin the quest by talking to the Little Orcy inside the tiny door in Shadow Desert (1).
Talk to Baby Orcy
Progress through the conversation until you receive Orcy's Pacy.
If you lose the pacifier you can get another one by returning to Baby Orcy.

From Aeven Mines, go north to Aeven Falls. At the north of Aeven Falls, you will find Pacy Cave, between the two water falls.
Click on the shrine in the middle of the cave to complete the quest.\
