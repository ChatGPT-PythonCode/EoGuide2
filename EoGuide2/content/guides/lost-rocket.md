Lost RocketLocation: Aeven North EntranceQuest Giver: Kid MiloLevel Requirement:  1Reward: 250 expGo to the Aeven North Entrance by the mountain lift, located north of Aeven.  Talk to Kid Milo to start the quest.Kid Milo explains he left his Toy Rocket on top of Aeven Top and asks you to retrieve it.Take the lift next to Kid Milo.Take the next lift to reach Aeven Top.Head all the way north and pick up the Toy Rocket from the platform.Return to Kid Milo.Give him the Toy Rocket, and he will reward you with 250 exp.
