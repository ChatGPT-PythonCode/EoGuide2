**Location:** Ancient Garden  
**Quest Giver:** Gardener  
**Level Requirement:** 26  
**Rewards:** 50,000 exp and 1000 eons

Garden Sweep is a quest given by Gardener in Ancient Garden. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Head to Ancient City and find the Gardener
2. If you're following the video below, you'll see the Gardener at timestamp 0:57
3. Otherwise, keep scrolling below to see the written instructions
4. To get there, take the lift at the Aeven North Entrance (near Kid Milo)
5. Head east and purchase an Air Ticket from Flynn Fly’s Airline Shop
6. Speak to Holly High at the Air Dock and select Ancient Airdock
7. You’ll arrive at the Ancient Airport
8. From there, go west one map, then north, and talk to the Gardener to start the quest
9. The Gardener explains that mushrooms in the northern cave are growing rapidly and asks you to help
10. Go north into the Ancient Garden North and defeat 100 Shrooms
11. *NOTE:
   Only the shrooms in the garden will count towards this quest
12. The ones within the cave and at the Schimmel boss room will not count
13. After defeating 100 Shrooms, return to the Gardener
   Garden Patrol
   which can be completed 1x a day for 12,000 exp and an Ancient Scroll
   Ancient City
   Turkey
   Garden Patrol (Daily)

## Completion

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **50,000 EXP, 1000 Eons**.
