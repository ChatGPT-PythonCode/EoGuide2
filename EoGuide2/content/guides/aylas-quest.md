Aeven
Ayla's QuestLocation: AevenQuest Giver: AylaLevel Requirement:  Level 3Reward: 2,200 exp and 100 eonsGo to the Aeven Witch Shop, located on the east side of Aeven.  Talk to Ayla, who is sitting outside, to start the quest.Ayla explains she is trying to join the Order of the Witches, but she forgot the final ingredient for her magic brew. She suggests you pose as a witch and speak to the Witch inside the shop.Enter the Aeven Witch Shop and talk to the Witch   During the dialogue, select the following options in order:·       Hello fellow witch.·       I studied witchcraft for years.·       What is the missing ingredient?·       Yes, I knew that all along.Exit the shop and speak with Ayla again.  She will explain that Moonpetals blossom at Aeven Top.Head to Aeven Top by going to the Aeven Top Lift, north of Aeven.  Take the next lift just north of you.Collect the Moonpetal from the ground.Return to Ayla outside the Witch Shop in Aeven.Ayla thanks you and rewards you with 2,200 exp and 100 eons.
