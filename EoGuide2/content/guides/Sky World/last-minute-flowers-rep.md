**Location:** Skywonder Airport  
**Quest Giver:** Dash Flower  
**Level Requirement:** 17  
**Rewards:** 2,700 exp and 36 eons

Last Minute Flowers (rep) is a quest given by Dash Flower in Skywonder Airport. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Head to the Skywonder Airport
2. If starting in Aeven, go north and take the elevator west of the Aeven Mines Entrance
3. Travel east and buy an Air Ticket from Flynn Fly
4. Then talk to Holly High and select Skyworld Airdock
5. Once you arrive at the Skywonder Airport map, head west until you find Dash Flower
6. Dash Flower asks you to collect 12 Skyflowers
7. Head north and gather 12 Skyflowers from the resource nodes
   Skywonder
   Turkey
   Fallen Picnic
   Last Minute Fur (rep)
   Sky Unclogged

## Completion

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **2,700 EXP, 36 Eons**.
