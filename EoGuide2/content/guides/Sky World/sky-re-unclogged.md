**Location:** Skywonder Toilets  
**Quest Giver:** Debby Skylar  
**Level Requirement:** 17  
**Rewards:** 42,000 exp and 300 eons

Sky Re-Unclogged is a quest given by Debby Skylar in Skywonder Toilets. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Skywonder
   Sky Re-Unclogged
   Skywonder Toilets
   Debby Skylar
   Level 17
   42,000 exp and 300 eons
2. Take the Aeven Airliner 121 to Skywonder
3. Talk with Debby Skylar inside Skywonder
4. Once off the boat, go to the main city
5. The toilet house is on the same map as the Skyworld Bank
6. Go inside and speak with Debby Skylar, she will ask you to find out whats wrong with the toilets
7. Accept the quest and then go back outside and walk behind the house
8. Once inside the toilets, walk south where you will find a clogged pipe
9. Wait 1-2 minutes for the fat rat to spawn, and then collect it

   Last Minute Flowers (rep)

## Completion

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **42,000 EXP, 300 Eons**.
