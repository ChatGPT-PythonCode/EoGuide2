**Location:** Skywonder Toilets  
**Quest Giver:** Debby Skylar  
**Level Requirement:** 1  
**Rewards:** 6,300 exp and 300 eons

Sky Unclogged is a quest given by Debby Skylar in Skywonder Toilets. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Skywonder
   Sky Unclogged
   Skywonder Toilets
   Debby Skylar
   Level 1
   6,300 exp and 300 eons
   140 Eons to buy the air ticket
2. Find the house in the far west of the map and enter it
3. Speak with Debby Skylar and she will tell you toilets are broken and ask you to take a look at them
4. Leave the house and go west behind it
5. Follow the map down the tunnel until you reach the end
6. Click on the stinky heap in the middle of the room to remove it, once you're done return to Debby to turn in the quest
   Skywonder
   Sky Re-Unclogged

## Completion

Upon completing the quest, you are rewarded with **6,300 EXP, 300 Eons**.
