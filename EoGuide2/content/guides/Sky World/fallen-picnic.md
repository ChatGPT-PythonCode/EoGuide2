**Location:** Skywonder Edge  
**Quest Giver:** Silly Skylar  
**Level Requirement:** 17  
**Rewards:** 69,000 exp and 500 eons

Fallen Picnic is a quest given by Silly Skylar in Skywonder Edge. Follow the steps below to complete the quest and earn your reward.

## Walkthrough

1. Head to the Skywonder Airport
2. From Aeven, go north and take the elevator west of the Aeven Mines Entrance
3. Travel east and buy an Air Ticket from Flynn Fly
4. Then talk to Holly High and select Skyworld Airdock
5. Once you arrive at Skywonder Airport, follow the map to reach Skywonder Edge and find Silly Skylar
6. Talk to Silly Skylar
7. He explains that he accidentally dropped a Lunchbag over the edge of the map and asks you to help find it
