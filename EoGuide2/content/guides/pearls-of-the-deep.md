Pearls of the DeepLocation: Atlantis Big OceanQuest Giver: Carl CoralLevel Requirement:  Level 26Reward: 90,000 exp1. Go to Atlantis and speak with Carl Coral just past the spawn point. and speak with him, and he will ask you to bring him 10 Ancient Pearls.
2. You can get pearls on two maps. One is the current map you are on now named Big Ocean and the second can be found to the far east inside the sinking ship.
Note: the pearl spawns in the big ocean take longer to respawn than the ones in the ship.
3. Once you have collected all 10 Ancient Pearls return to Carl Coral to complete the quest.
