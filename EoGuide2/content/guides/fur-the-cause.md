## Walkthrough

Fur the Cause

Note:
2. Go to Wacku Deep West by first going to the Aeven Port and talking to the Boat Captain and travel to Wacku Wilds Port
3. Once you're off the boat go north until your off the docks then head west until you reach the cave
4. Enter the cave then travel to the west side and cross the wooden bridge
5. Once leaving the cave head west and find Furry Furgy
6. He will ask you to bring him 10 Ape Fur
7. Walk the map killing apes until you have collected 10 ape fur
8. Warning
- Apes will agro onto you if they see you attacking another Ape next to them
9. Once you have collected all 10 return to Furry Furgy to claim your reward off 90,000 exp
