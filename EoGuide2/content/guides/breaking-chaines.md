Josand
Breaking ChainesLocation: Ironguard TentQuest Giver: Keal IronbreakerLevel Requirement:  Level 26Reward: Leave Iron Man ModeNote:  This is the only way to remove Iron Man Mode from your character.The quest Shabby Repair is a prerequisite to start this quest. 		Go to Josland and travel north to the Josand Ironguard Map.Enter the Ironguard Tent on the west side of the camp.Speak with Keal Ironbreaker.He'll give you two warnings before you can accept losing your Iron Man Mode.After accepting to remove Iron Man Mode your character will now be in default mode.  You can now trade, party and participate in the game as you would normally.
