Aeven
ShowtimeLocation: Aeven TheatreQuest Giver: Aeven MagicianLevel Requirement:  Level 1Reward: 2000 exp Go to the Aeven Theatre and talk to the Aeven Magician to start the quest. (Aeven Theatre is where the Bank used to be)He has a lot of dialog, click through it until he asks you to meet him on the Main Stage.Go through the doors east of the Aeven Magician.He will ask you to follow him to the stage.Talk to him again and he will ask you to be his assistant.The Aeven Magician's first request is to go to the backstage and search the chest for his Bunny.Return to him and tell him you can't find the BunnyHis next request is for you to go to the backstage and go through the floor-hatch.Return to the Aeven Magician and tell him the bad news.After the crowd leaves talk to the Aeven Manager again.  He'll ask you to meet him in the Main Lobby again.Go to the Main Lobby and talk to the Aeven Magician to claim your reward of 2,000 exp.
